Order under Section 78(6)

Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

 

File Number: SWL-57581-22

 

 

In the matter of:

807, 1201 RICHMOND STREET N LONDON ON N6A3L6

 

Between:

Homestead Land Holdings Limited

Landlord

 

 

and

 

 

Emily-Jayne Richards

Tenant

 

 

 

Homestead Land Holdings Limited (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Emily-Jayne Richards (the 'Tenant') and for an order to have the Tenant pay the rent the Tenant owes because the Tenant failed to meet a condition specified in the order issued by the Board on August 6, 2021 with respect to application SWL-47161-20.

 

Determinations:

 

1.     The order provided that the Landlord could apply to the Board under section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act') without notice to the Tenant to terminate the tenancy and evict the Tenant if the Tenant did not meet certain condition(s) specified in the order.

 

2.     I find that the Tenant has not met the following conditions specified in the order. The Tenant did not pay $1226.88 arrears on or before December 15, 2021. The Tenant did not pay $1867.14 rent on or before January 1, 2022.

 

3.     The previous application included a request for an order for the payment of arrears of rent. The resulting order required the Tenant to pay rent or some or all of the arrears of rent. Accordingly, in addition to eviction, the Landlord is entitled to request an order for the payment of arrears of rent and compensation.

 

4.     The Tenant was ordered to pay $7,361.26 for rent arrears and the costs related to the Landlord's application fee in Order SWL-47161-20. The amount that is still owing from that order is $2,455.50 and that amount is included in this order. As a result, the previous order SWL-47161-20 is cancelled.

 

5.     Since the date of the order, the Tenant has failed to pay the full rent that became owing for the period from January 1, 2022 to January 31, 2022.

 

6.     The Landlord collected a rent deposit of $1,852.84 from the Tenant and this deposit is still being held by the Landlord.

 

7.     Interest on the rent deposit is owing to the Tenant for the period from October 1, 2021 to January 26, 2022.

 

It is ordered that:

 

1.     Order SWL-47161-20 is cancelled.

 

2.     The tenancy between the Landlord and the Tenant is terminated. The Tenant must move out of the rental unit on or before February 6, 2022.

 

3.     The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $2,178.68*. This amount represents the rent owing up to January 26, 2022 and the costs related to the application fee for the previous application, less the rent deposit and interest the Landlord owes on the rent deposit.

 

4.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $61.39 per day for compensation for the use of the unit starting January 27, 2022 to the date the Tenant moves out of the unit.

 

5.     If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord the full amount owing* on or before February 6, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 7, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.

 

6.     If the unit is not vacated on or before February 6, 2022, then starting February 7, 2022, the Landlord may file this order with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the eviction may be enforced.

 

7.     Upon receipt of this order, the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after February 7, 2022.
