King East Developments Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring Krisztian Lakatos and Vivian Lakatos (the 'Tenants') to pay
the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by way
of video conference on January 10, 2022. The
Landlord’s legal representative Howard
Levenson attended the hearing. As of 3:10pm
the Tenants were not
present despite being served with notice of hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.      
The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenants did not pay the total
rent they were required to pay for the period from July 1, 2021 to September
30, 2021. Since filing the application, additional rent came due for
the months of October and November 2021.
 
3.     The Tenants vacated
the rental unit on November
11, 2021.
 
4.     The Tenants made no payments
since filing the application.
 
5.     The Landlord is not holding
a last month’s rent deposit.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord $9,595.61, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to November 11, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: TNL-34822-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before February
25, 2022, the Tenants
will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from February 26,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
