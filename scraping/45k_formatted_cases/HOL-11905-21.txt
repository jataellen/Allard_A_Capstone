Savic Holdings Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to
terminate the tenancy and evict Rheal Therrien (the 'Tenant') because the
Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on December 16, 2021. Only the Landlord
attended the hearing. As of
2:39 p.m., the Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing although
properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant has not paid
the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period from July 1, 2021 to November 30, 2021. Because
of the arrears, the Landlord
served a Notice of Termination.
 
2.    
The Tenant
vacated the rental
unit on November
30, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent was
$1,800.00.
 
4.     The Landlord is not holding
a last month's rent deposit.
 
5.     The Tenant has made no payments since
the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant
vacated the rental
unit on November
30, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenant shall pay to
the Landlord $7,725.00*, which represents the amount of rent owing and
compensation up to November 30, 2021.




File Number: HOL-11905-21
 
3.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenant does not
pay the Landlord the full amount owing* on or before February 5, 2022, the
Tenant will start to owe interest. This
will be simple interest calculated from February 6, 2022 at 2.00% annually on
the balance outstanding.
