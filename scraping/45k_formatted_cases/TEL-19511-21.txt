Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: TEL-19511-21
 
 
In the matter of:      60 BENTONS LANE
MILFORD ON K0K2P0
 
Between:                  William Goodman                                    Landlord
 
 
and
 




Michelle Martier Paul Vanhecke


Tenants




 
 
 
William Goodman (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Michelle Martier and Paul Vanhecke (the
'Tenants') because the Landlord requires possession of the rental unit for the
purpose of residential occupation.
 
This application was heard via videoconference on January 25, 2022.
 
The Landlord, William Goodman,
The Tenant, Michelle
Martier, and the Tenant Paul Vanhecke
attended the hearing.
 
 
Determinations:
 
Preliminary Issue:
 
1.       
At the hearing, the Landlord
testified that he did not pay compensation equal to one month’s rent to
the Tenant on or before
the termination date noted on the N12 Notice to End the
Tenancy because the Landlord’s Spouse Requires the Unit.
 
2.       
Section 48.1 of the Act provides
that a landlord shall compensate a tenant in an amount
equal to one month’s rent if the landlord gives the tenant a notice of
termination of the tenancy under section 48.
 
3.       
Section 55.1 of the Act requires
that this compensation be paid no later than the
termination date in the notice served.
 
4.       
Because the Landlord failed
to comply with section 55.1 of the Act, the Board
has no authority to terminate
the tenancy under these circumstances.
 
5.       
This order contains all the reasons
in this matter and no further reasons
will be issued.




File Number: TEL-19511-21
 
 
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.       
The Landlord's application is dismissed.
