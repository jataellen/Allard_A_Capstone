King Edward Apartments (the
'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Larry Marshall
(the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant failed to meet a condition
specified in the order
issued by the Board on December 14, 2021 with respect to application
SWL-53832-21.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The order provided that the Landlord could apply to
the Board under section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act')
without notice to the Tenant
to terminate the tenancy and evict the Tenant if the Tenant
did not meet certain condition(s) specified in the
order.
 
 
2.     I find that the Tenant has not met the following
condition specified in the order: On or about
December 10, 2021 the Tenant
inferred with the reasonable enjoyment of the unit for other tenants by playing loud
music.
 
 
It is ordered that:
 
 
1.     The tenancy between
the Landlord and the Tenant
is terminated. The Tenant must move
out of the rental unit on or before February 4, 2022.
 
 
2.     If the unit is not vacated on or before
February 4, 2022, then starting
February 5, 2022, the Landlord may file this order
with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the eviction may be
enforced.




File Number: SWL-57540-22
 
3.    
Upon receipt of this order,
the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after February 5, 2022.
