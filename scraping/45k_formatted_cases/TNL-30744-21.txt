Nanan Pathania
(the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Sophia
Mororano, Kiev Salvatierra, Clarissa Schopp, Thomas
Zoschke and Abigail Fiawornu (the 'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 8, 2021.
Only the Landlord attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants
were in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on April 30, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenants
did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period from
December 1, 2020 to April 30, 2021.
 
4.    
The Tenants
have been credited
with the rent deposit in the amount of $5,400.00
was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$5,325.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to April 30, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenants
shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: TNL-30744-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not
pay the Landlord the full amount owing on or before January 18, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from January 19,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
