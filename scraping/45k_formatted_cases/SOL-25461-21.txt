Almar Knowles

Tenants



 
 
 
192 Hughson Street Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring
Almar Knowles and Malik
Forrester (the 'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on February 1, 2022. Only
the Landlord’s Legal Representative, John Edlund, attended the hearing. As of
2:25 p.m., the Tenants were not present or represented at the hearing
although properly served
with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants
did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period from July 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants are in possession of the rental unit.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent is $1,425.69.
 
4.     The Tenants have made no payments since the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$4,101.38, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SOL-25461-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not
pay the Landlord the full amount owing on
or before February 19, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from February 20,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
