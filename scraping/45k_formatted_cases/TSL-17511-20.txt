Timbercreek Asset Management Inc (the 'Landlord') applied for an order
to terminate the tenancy of Andrew
Brown (the 'Tenant'), and David
Fleming (the “Unauthorized Occupant”) because the Tenant transferred the
tenancy to the Unauthorized Occupant without the Landlord’s consent.
The Landlord
also applied for compensation for each day the Unauthorized Occupants remain in the rental unit.
 
This application
was heard by videoconference on October 12, 2021. The Landlord was represented Vinuri Sivalingam. As of 11:35 a.m. neither the Tenant nor
the Unauthorized Occupant present or represented at the hearing
although properly served with notice of this
hearing as it appears from the Certificate of Service.
 
Ronen Bacher
appeared as a witness for the Landlord.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The application, filed
August 27, 2020, is based on the unauthorized transfer of tenancy by the Tenant to David
Fleming, as discovered on July 30,
2020. The Tenant transferred the occupancy of the rental unit to DAVID FLEMING (hereinafter called the 'Unauthorized Occupant') in a manner that
was not authorized by the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act').
The Landlord did not enter into a tenancy agreement with this person.
 
2.    
It is the undisputed evidence of the Landlord’s witness
that he conducted
a wellness check at the
rental unit and discovered the that the Unauthorized Occupant, David Fleming,
was in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.    
The Unauthorized Occupant moved out of the rental unit on March 15, 2021.
 
4.    
Rent was not paid on the unit between
July 30, 2020 and the date the Unauthorized
Occupant moved out of the rental unit on March 15, 2021.
 
5.    
The lawful
monthly rent is
$1,146.00.




File Number: TSL-17511-20
 
 
 
6.    
The Landlord
is entitled to compensation for the use and occupation of the rental
unit by the Unauthorized
Occupant.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The tenancy
between the Landlord
and the Tenant is terminated as of March 15, 2021.
 
2.    
The Unauthorized Occupant shall pay to the Landlord $8,624.88, which represents
compensation for the use of the unit from July 30, 2020 to March 15, 2021.
 
3.    
The Unauthorized Occupant shall pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing by January
16, 2022.
 
4.    
If the Unauthorized Occupant does not pay
the Landlord the full amount owing by January
16, 2022, he will owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from January 17,
2022 at 2.00% on the outstanding balance.
