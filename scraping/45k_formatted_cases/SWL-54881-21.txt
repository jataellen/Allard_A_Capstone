Galaxy Value Add Ontario Properties Lp (the 'Landlord') applied for an order
requiring Nicole Luna (the 'Tenant') to pay the rent that
the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on December 20, 2021. Only the Landlord's Legal Representative Tricia Radam attended the hearing. As of 10:56 a.m., the Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing
although properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant did not pay
the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period from August 1,
2021 to September 30, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant is in possession of the rental
unit.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent is $858.32.
 
4.    
The Tenants
made no payments after the application was
filed.
 
5.    
The only part of the
application that proceeded was the claim for rent arrears. As a result, the
order will be limited to rent arrears and costs only, not eviction.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant
shall pay to the Landlord
$848.93, which represents the amount of rent owing up to September 30, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SWL-54881-21
 
3.     If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord the
full amount owing on or before February 13, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe
interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 14, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
