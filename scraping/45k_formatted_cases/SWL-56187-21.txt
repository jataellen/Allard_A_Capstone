Galaxy Value Add Ontario
Properties LP (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Sherly Mathew (the 'Tenant') to pay the
rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by video conference on February 3, 2022.
 
Only the Landlord's Legal
Representative, Sara Mathew, attended the hearing. As of 2:12 p.m. the Tenant
was not present or represented at the hearing
although properly served
with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The
Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant
was required to pay for the period
from September 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
2.     The Tenant is in possession of the rental
unit.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent is $1,250.00.
 
4.     The Tenant has made no payments since the application was filed.
 
5.     The
Landlord’s Legal Representative submitted the Landlord
had sold the property and therefore was only seeking
an order for arrears of rent up to the effective date, October
28, 2021.
It is
ordered that:
 
1.     The
Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $2,400.80, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 28, 2021.
2.    
The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SWL-56187-21
 
3.     If the Tenant does not pay the
Landlord the full amount owing on or before March 7, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest.
This will be simple interest
calculated from March 8, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
