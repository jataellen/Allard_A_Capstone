Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

737 FAIRDALE AVE OTTAWA ON K1G1X6

Dimitri Garaschenko
 


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Donna Cyr
Rene Marc Wistaff


Tenants



 
Dimitri Garaschenko (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring Rene Marc Wistaff
and Donna Cyr (the 'Tenants')
to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference
on October 26, 2021. Only the
Landlord’s Legal Representative, John McDonell, attended the hearing. As of
2:15 p.m., the Tenants were not present or represented at the hearing
although properly served
with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants
did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period from April 1, 2020 to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants are in possession of the rental unit.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent is $2,400.00.
 
4.     The Tenants paid $19,100.00, after the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$4,800.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: EAL-95139-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not pay the
Landlord the full amount owing on or before
March 7, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated
from March 8, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
