Order under Section
78(6)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: SWL-56739-21-EX
In the matter
of:      310, 151 YORK STREET
LONDON ON N6A1A8
 
Between:                  Ams Properties Inc                                    Landlord
 
and
 
Matthew Madden-Molyneau                                                                                                                     Tenant
 
On November 8,
2021, Ams Properties Inc (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the
tenancy and evict Matthew Madden-Molyneau (the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant failed
to meet a condition specified in the order issued
by the Board on September 2, 2021 with respect to application SWL-52408-21.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The order
does not provide
that the Landlord
can apply to the Board under section
78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 to terminate the tenancy
and evict the Tenant if the
Tenant did not meet certain condition(s) specified in the order.
 
2.    
The order does provide
that the Landlord
may ask the Board to reopen the application.
This request must be filed no later than 30 days after the Tenant’s breach.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Landlord's application under section
78 of the Act is dismissed.
 
2.    
Order SWL-52408-21, issued on September
2, 2021 remains in effect.
