Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies
Act, 2006
 
File Number: TNL-04675-18



 


 


 



 
 
CLP (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy
and evict EM and EM (the 'Tenants') because the Tenants did not pay the rent
that the Tenants owe.
 
This application was heard in Toronto on May 31, 2018.
 
The Landlord’s representative, FQ and the
Tenant, (EM), attended the hearing. Also in attendance was EM’s daughter, HB,
and she assisted EM with translation. 
 
Determinations:
 
1.     
The Tenants have not paid
the total rent the Tenants were required to pay for the period from March 1,
2018 to May 31, 2018.  Because of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of
Termination effective April 27, 2018.
2.     
The monthly rent as of the
date of the hearing is $1,263.75.
3.     
The Tenants have lived in
the unit for almost five years. EM says that she receives monthly benefits from
(OW) in the amount of $2,100.00. According to EM, the OW payments stopped in
March 2018 because OW moved offices and lost her case file. She has had
difficulty contacting her case worker to resolve the matter. 
4.     
The Tenants requested relief
and proposed a payment plan for the arrears and June 2018 rent. The plan would
see the arrears paid in approximately nine months.
5.     
The Landlord opposed any
relief and the length of the payment plan and requested a standard order. 
6.     
I have considered all of the
disclosed circumstances in accordance with subsection 83(2) of the Residential
Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act'), and find that it would not be unfair to
grant relief from eviction subject to  the condition(s) set out in this order
pursuant to subsection 83(1)(a) and 204(1) of the Act. I considered the length
of the tenancy and will give the Tenants an opportunity to continue the tenancy
by paying the arrears according to the plan they proposed.
It is ordered that:

Landlord $2,761.25, which represents the arrears of rent owing up to May 31,
2018 ($2,571.25), plus the Landlord’s application filing fee ($190.00).
