Order under Section 135
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: SOT-17516-20
 
 
In the matter of:      39 FLADD COURT
ELORA ON N0B1S0
 




Between:                  Constance Mcdonald Stephen
Mcdonald


Tenants




 
 
and
 




Gina Brusch Lionel Jorge


Landlords




 
 
 
Constance Mcdonald and Stephen Mcdonald
(the 'Tenants') applied
for an order determining that Lionel Jorge and Gina Brusch (the
'Landlords') have collected or retained money illegally.
 
This application was scheduled by videoconference on October 13, 2021 at 9am.
Only the Tenants attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    The Landlords failed to pay the
Tenants interest on last month's
deposit as required
by the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act').
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.       
The Landlords shall pay to the Tenants the sum of $2,766.60. This amount represents
 
$166.60 for interest on the last month's rent deposit and $2,600.00 in compensation
for moving out.
2.       
If the Landlords
do not pay the Tenants the full amount owing by January 21, 2022, the Landlords will owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from January
22, 2022 at 2.00% annually on
the outstanding balance.




File Number: SOT-17516-20
 
3.       
The
Tenants have the right, at any time, to collect
the full amount
owing or any balance outstanding under this order.
