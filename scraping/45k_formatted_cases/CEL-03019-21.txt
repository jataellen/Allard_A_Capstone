Order under
Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: CEL-03019-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


17 BIRD STREET BARRIE ON L4N0X4


 




Between:


Shawn Still


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Doug English


Tenant



 
 
 
Shawn Still (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring Doug English (the 'Tenant') to pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on January 17, 2022.
Only the Landlord attended the
hearing. As of 3:06 p.m., the Tenant was not present or represented at the
hearing although properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from April 1, 2020 to January 31, 2022.
 
3.     The monthly rent is $1,475.00.
 
4.     The Tenant has made no payments since the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $31,450.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to January 31, 2022.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before February
4, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 5, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
