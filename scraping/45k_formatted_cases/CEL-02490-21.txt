Order under Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: CEL-02490-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


301, 1 BERNICK DR BARRIE ON L4M5K1


 




Between:


1 Bernick Drive
Holdings Limited


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


James Lemaitre Stephanie Edmar


Tenants



 
 
 
1 Bernick
Drive Holdings Limited
(the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Stephanie Edmar and James Lemaitre (the 'Tenants') to pay the rent that
they owe. The Landlord also claimed administration charges related to NSF
cheques.
 
This application
was heard by videoconference on December 6, 2021. Only the Landlord's Agent, Bryan Rubin attended the hearing. As of 1:43 p.m., the Tenant was not
present or represented at the hearing although
properly served with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.      The Tenants did not pay the total rent they were required to pay for the period
from August 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
 
2.      The Tenants vacated the rental unit on
September 30, 2021. The Tenants were in
possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.     
The application is amended to reflect the rent owing
up to the end of September 30, 2021.
 
4.     
The lawful
monthly rent was $1,536.01.
 
5.     
The Landlord
is not holding a last month's rent deposit.
 
6.      The Landlord incurred charges
of $20.00 for administration charges
related to cheques tendered
by or on behalf of the Tenants, which were returned NSF.
 
7.     
The Tenants
paid $1,536.01 after
the application was filed.




File Number: CEL-02490-21
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on September 30, 2021.
The Tenants were in
possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$1,536.01, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to September 30, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenants
shall also pay to the Landlord $20.00,
which represents the total charges related to NSF cheques tendered
to the Landlord by or on behalf of the Tenants
 
4.    
The Tenants
shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
5.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord the
full amount owing on or before January 15, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest.
This will be simple interest
calculated from January 16,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
