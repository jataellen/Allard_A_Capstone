Esmeralda Backa Ilir Backa


Tenants



 
 
 
2030251 Ontario
Inc (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring
Ilir Backa and Esmeralda
Backa (the 'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on December 6, 2021. Only the Landlord's Legal Representative, Bryan Rubin
attended the hearing. As of 10:35
a.m., the Tenants were not present or represented at the hearing although
properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants
did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period from
November 1, 2020 to September 30, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on September 30, 2021. The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the
date the application was filed.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent was $1,662.82.
 
4.     The Landlord is not holding
a last month's rent deposit.
 
5.     The Tenants have made no payments since the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on September 30, 2021.
The Tenants were in
possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$2,137.10, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to September 30, 2021.




File Number: TNL-29204-20
 
3.    
The Tenants
shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord the
full amount owing on or before January 8, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from January 9,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
