Order under
Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: HOL-12111-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


9, 55 PROVENCE TRAIL TORONTO ON M6V5B6


 




Between:


Nivlog Investments Limited


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Jennie Gallant


Tenant



 
 
 
Nivlog Investments Limited (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Jennie
Gallant (the 'Tenant') to pay
the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on January 10, 2022. Only the Landlord's Legal Representative, Debra Fine,
attended the hearing. As of 2:41 p.m.,
the Tenant was not present or
represented at the hearing although properly served with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on
September 15, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was
filed.
 
2.     The lawful monthly
rent is $2,030.00.
 
3.     The Tenant made no payments
after the application was filed.
 
4.    
The Landlord collected a rent deposit
of $2,030.00 from the Tenant and this deposit will be applied towards the outstanding
arrears.
 
5.    
Interest on the rent deposit has been paid to the Tenant for the period
from January 1, 2020 to December 31, 2020.
 
6.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant
was required to pay,
for the period from August
1, 2021 to September 15, 2021, in the amount of $2,978.61.
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy is terminated as of September 15, 2021, the date the Tenant gave vacant
possession of the rental unit to the Landlord.




File Number: HOL-12111-21
 
2.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $948.61, which represents the amount of rent owing up to September 15, 2021, less the
rent deposit.
 
3.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.     If the Tenant
does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before January
29, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from January 30, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
