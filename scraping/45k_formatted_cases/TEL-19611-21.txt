Mirali Nadjafi
(the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Julie
Stevenson and Rocky (Steve) Stevenson
(the 'Tenants') because
the Tenants did not pay the
rent that the Tenants owe.
 
This application
was heard by videoconference on January 5, 2022. The Landlord’s representatives,
Carrie Aylwin and Farideh Bakhshoodeh, attended the hearing.
As of 2:29 p.m., the
Tenants were not present or represented at the hearing although properly served
with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants
have not paid the total rent the Tenants were required to pay for the period from April 15, 2021 to January 14, 2022. Because
of the arrears, the Landlord
served a Notice of
Termination effective September 19, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on December
9, 2021. The Tenants were in
possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent was
$3,000.00.
 
4.    
The Tenants
have made no payments since
the application was filed.
 
5.    
The Landlord
collected a rent deposit of $2,400.00 from the Tenants
and this deposit
is still being held by the Landlord.
 
6.    
Interest on the rent deposit is owing to the Tenants
for the period from April
7, 2020 to December 31, 2020.




File Number: TEL-19611-21
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant vacated the rental unit on
December 9, 2021. The Tenants
were in possession of the
rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$11,376.68*, which represents the amount of rent
owing and compensation up to December 9, 2021,
less the rent deposit and
interest the Landlord owes on the rent deposit.
 
3.    
The Tenants
shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord
the full amount owing* on or before January 28, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from January 29,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
