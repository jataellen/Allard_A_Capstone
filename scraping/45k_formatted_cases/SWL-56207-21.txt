Galaxy Value Add Ontario
Properties LP (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Resimol Poulose and Saiju Augustine (the
'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by video conference on February 3, 2022.
 
Only the Landlord's Legal
Representative, Sara Mathew, attended the hearing. As of 2:17 p.m. the Tenants
were not present
or represented at the hearing
although properly served
with notice of this hearing by
the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     
The Tenants did not pay the total rent they were required to pay for the period
from August 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
2.     
The Tenants are in possession of the rental
unit.
3.     
The lawful monthly rent is $960.68.
4.     
The Tenants have made no payments
since the application was filed.
5.     
The Landlord’s Legal Representative submitted the Landlord
had sold the property and therefore was only seeking
an order for arrears of rent up to the effective date, October
28, 2021.
It is ordered that:
 
1.     
The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord $2,805.60, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 28, 2021.
2.     
The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SWL-56207-21
 
3.     
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before March 7, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe
interest. This will be simple
interest calculated from March 8, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance
outstanding.
