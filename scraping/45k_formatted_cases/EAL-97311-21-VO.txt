Osgoode Properties


Landlord

Yandhi
  Cranddent


Tenant



 
 
On December 17,
2021 the Board issued order EAL-97311-21 terminating the tenancy and evicting Yandhi
Cranddent (the 'Tenant')
because the Tenant
failed to pay rent that the Tenant owes.
 
On January 5,
2022 the Tenant filed a motion to void order EAL-97311-21 because before the
eviction order became enforceable, the Tenant paid Osgoode Properties (the
'Landlord') the amount required to void the order under subsection 74(4) of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the
'Act').
 
Determinations:
 
1.    The
Tenant filed an affidavit swearing that the Tenant paid the full amount
required to void order EAL-97311-21 to the Landlord
before the eviction
order became enforceable.
 
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The motion to void order EAL-97311-21 is
granted.
 
2.     Order EAL-97311-21 is void and cannot be enforced by the Landlord.
