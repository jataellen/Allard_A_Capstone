Review Order
 
980612 Ontario Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Silas Martin (the 'Tenant') because the
Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was resolved by order EAL-91858-20 issued on March 30, 2021. On April 23, 2021, the Tenant
requested a review of the order.
The request was heard on June 16, 2021, and the request for a review
was granted in order EAL- 91858-20-RV-IN2, issued on July 23,
2021. The matter was adjourned to be
heard de novo.
 
The review was heard on January
27, 2022.
 
 
Only the Tenant’s legal representative, M. Thiele, attended
the hearing.
 
 
Determinations:
 
 
1.     Neither the Landlord
nor the Landlord’s legal representative attended the hearing.
 
2.    
The Tenant’s legal representative
said that he had been advised that the Landlord was seeking to withdraw the L1 application. During the hearing,
he said that he received
an email from the Landlord’s legal representative, K. Brennan, stating
that the Landlord requested to withdraw the L1 application.
 
3.     The Tenant consented
to the withdrawal of the Landlord’s application.




 
It is ordered that:
 
 
1.     The Landlord’s L1 application is dismissed.
