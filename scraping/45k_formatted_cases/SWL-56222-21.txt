Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

of:      408, 750 KIPPS LANE
LONDON ON N5Y4V4
 
Between:                  Galaxy Value Add Ontario
Properties LP                                    Landlord
 
 
and
 




Phul Maya Rai Usha Hang Rai


Tenants




 
 
Galaxy Value Add Ontario
Properties LP (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Phul Maya Rai and Usha Hang Rai (the
'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on February
3, 2022. The Landlord's Legal Representative, Sara Mathew, and the Tenant, Usha Hang
Rai, attended the hearing.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenants did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period from May 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants paid $859.25 after
the application was filed.
 
3.     The only amount
outstanding is part of the Landlord's cost of filing
the application. As a
result.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord
$182.19 for the Landlord’s cost of filing the
application.
 
2.     If the Tenants
do not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before March 7, 2022, they will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated
from March 8, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
