Skyline Living (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Marc Adelstein (the 'Tenant') because the
Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 23, 2021.
Only the Landlord’s representative, B. Casnaw, attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant was in
possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on October
31, 2021
 
3.    
The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
July 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
4.     The Tenant paid $1,045.02 after the application was filed.
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the
Landlord $1,042.56*, which represents the amount of rent owing and compensation up to October
31, 2021, less the rent deposit and interest the Landlord owes on the rent deposit.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: EAL-97272-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before February
12, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 13, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
