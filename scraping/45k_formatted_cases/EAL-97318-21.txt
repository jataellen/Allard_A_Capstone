Order under Section 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

In the matter

4, 381 TILLBURY AVENUE OTTAWA ON K2A0Y3


 




Between:


Carling Court/manor Park Mgmt Inc.


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


John Walsh


Tenant



 
 
 
Carling Court/manor Park Mgmt Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate
the tenancy and evict John
Walsh (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application
was heard by videoconference on November 23, 2021. Only the Landlord’s representative, N. Lamoureux, attended
the hearing. Determinations:
1.     The Tenant was in
possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on September 30, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
May 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the
Landlord $2,427.80*, which represents the amount of rent owing and compensation up to September
30, 2021, less the rent deposit and interest the Landlord owes on the rent deposit.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: EAL-97318-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before February
19, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 20, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
