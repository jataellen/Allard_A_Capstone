Order under Section 100
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: TSL-18798-20
 
 



In the matter
of:


5, 20 LOUISA STREET ETOBICOKE ON M8V2K6


 




Between:


Vinka Kovacic 2002094 Ontario Inc.


Landlords




 


 
and


 




 


Gray Quinton


Current Occupant



 
 
Vinka Kovacic and 2002094 Ontario Inc. (the 'Landlords')
applied for an order to terminate the tenancy of Melissa Mckenna
(the 'Tenant'), and for compensation for the use of the rental unit.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 16, 2021.
 
The Landlord V. Kovacic
and the Landlord Representative B. Knezevic attended
the hearing. The Current
Occupant did not attend the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     At the hearing,
the Landlord advised
that the Tenant
passed away on September 6, 2020.
 
2.     The Landlords application was filed on October 16, 2020.     Monthly rent was $1086.00
 
3.     The Tenant transferred the occupancy of the rental
unit to Gray Quinton
(hereinafter called the 'Unauthorized Occupant') in a manner that was
not authorized by the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act').
 
4.     The Landlords did not enter into a tenancy agreement with this person.
 
5.    
The Unauthorized Occupant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
6.     The Unauthorized Occupant
vacated the rental
unit on July 12, 2021. No rent was paid from the Unauthorized Occupant to
the Landlords.
 
7.     The Landlords is entitled to compensation for the use and occupation of the rental
unit by the Unauthorized
Occupant.




File Number: TSL-18798-20
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy between
the Landlords and the Tenant
is terminated as of July 12, 2021.
 
2.     The Unauthorized Occupant
shall pay to the Landlords $11,067.00, which represents compensation for the use of
the unit from September 6, 2020 to July 12, 2021.
 
3.     The Unauthorized Occupant
shall pay the Landlords the full amount
owing by February 20, 2022.
 
4.     If the Unauthorized Occupant does not pay the
Landlords the full amount owing by February 20, 2022, he will owe interest.
This will be simple interest
calculated from February 21,
2022 at 2.00% on the outstanding balance.
