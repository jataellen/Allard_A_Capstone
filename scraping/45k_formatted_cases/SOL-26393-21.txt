Order under Section 77
Residential Tenancies
Act, 2006
 
File Number: SOL-26393-21
 
 



In the
  matter of:


5 MCCAMMON STREET PARIS ON N3L1V8


 




Between:


Gary Donald Shannon Brian Edward North


Landlords




 


 
and


 




 


James Ernest Stanton Jessica Leigh Stratford


Tenants



 
 
 
Gary Donald
Shannon and Brian Edward North (the 'Landlords') applied for an order to terminate
the tenancy and evict James Ernest Stanton and
Jessica Leigh Stratford (the 'Tenants') because the Tenants entered into an agreement to terminate the
tenancy.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Landlords and the Tenants
signed an agreement
to terminate the tenancy as of
February 1, 2022.
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy between
the Landlords and the Tenants
is terminated. The Tenants must move out of the rental unit on or
before February 1, 2022.
 
2.     If the unit is not vacated on or before
February 1, 2022, then starting
February 2, 2022, the
Landlords may file this order with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the eviction
may be enforced.
 
3.    
Upon receipt of this order,
the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlords on or after February 2, 2022.
