69
Residential Tenancies
Act, 2006

Between:


Effi Serge


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Cody John Bruce
Card Sara Carol Martin


Tenants



 
 
 
Effi Serge (the
'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Cody John
Bruce Card and Sara Carol
Martin (the 'Tenants') because the Tenants
did not pay the rent that
the Tenants owe.
 
This application was heard via videoconference on November 25, 2021.
 
The Landlord’s Legal Representative Pierre-Vallery Wandji, the Landlord
and the Tenant Cody Card
attended the hearing.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants have not
paid the total rent the Tenants were required to pay for the period from June 1, 2021 to November
30, 2021. Because
of the arrears, the Landlord
served a Notice of Termination
effective July 24, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit as of November 1, 2021. The Tenant Cody Card
was in possession of the rental unit on the date application was filed.
 
3.    
The monthly
rent is $1,675.00
 
4.    
The Landlord
is not holding a rent deposit.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    The tenancy
between the Landlord
and the Tenant is terminated as of November
1, 2021, the date the Tenant
provided vacant possession to the Landlord.




File Number: EAL-96707-21
 
1.    
The Tenant
shall pay to the Landlord
$10,020.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing and compensation up to October 9, 2020, less the rent deposit and
interest the Landlord owes on the rent deposit.
 
2.    
The Tenant
shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing
the application.
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount owing* on or before
January 22, 2022, the Tenant
will start to owe interest. This will
be simple interest calculated from January 23, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
