Pinedale
Properties Ltd. (the 'Landlord') applied for an order
to terminate the tenancy and evict
Trenae Harvey and Keah Beazley
(the 'Tenants') because
the Tenants did not pay the rent that
the Tenants owe.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on January 12, 2022. The
Landlord's Legal Representative, Gail Kukor Lang, and the Tenants’
Representatives, Whitney Farrell
and Derron Geddes, attended
the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The parties consent
to remove Whitney Farrell
and Derron Geddes as Tenants to the application.
 
2.     The Tenants have not paid the total rent the Tenants were required to pay for the period from June 1, 2021 to January 31,
2022. Because of the arrears, the
Landlord served a Notice of Termination effective September 7, 2021.
 
3.     The Tenants vacated
the rental unit on
October 31, 2021. The Tenants
were in possession of the
rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
4.     The lawful monthly
rent was $2,509.00.
 
5.     The Tenants have made no payments since the application was filed.
 
6.    
The Landlord collected a rent deposit
of $2,509.00 from the Tenants
and this deposit
is still being held by the Landlord.
 
7.     Interest on the rent deposit has been paid to the Tenants
for the period from June 9, 2020 to May 31, 2021.




File Number: TSL-24276-21
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenants vacated
the rental unit on
October 31, 2021. The Tenants
were in possession of the
rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord
$3,152.11*, which represents the amount of rent
owing and compensation up to October 31, 2021, less the rent deposit.
 
3.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.     If the Tenants
do not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing* on or before March 1, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe
interest. This will be simple
interest calculated from March 2,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
