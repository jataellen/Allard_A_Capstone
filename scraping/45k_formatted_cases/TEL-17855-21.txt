Inez Knight (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the
tenancy and evict Sarah Hanley and Keith Hanley (the 'Tenants') because the
Tenants did not pay the rent that the Tenants owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on December 20, 2021. Only the Landlord
attended the hearing. As of 2:22 p.m., the Tenants were not present
or represented at the hearing
although properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants have not
paid the total rent the Tenants were required to pay for the period from June
1, 2021 to December 31, 2021. Because of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of
Termination.
 
2.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on August 31, 2021. The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent was
$1,872.70
 
4.    
The Tenants
have made no payments since the application was filed.
 
5.    
The Landlord
collected a rent deposit of $1,800.00 from the Tenants
and this deposit
is still being held by the
Landlord.
 
6.    
Interest on the rent deposit is owing to the Tenants
for the period from September
15, 2015 to December 31,
2020.




File Number: TEL-17855-21
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on August 31, 2021. The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenants shall pay
to the Landlord $3,636.65*, which represents the amount of rent owing and
compensation up to August 31, 2021, less the rent deposit and interest the
Landlord owes on the rent deposit.
 
3.    
The Tenants
shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenants do not
pay the Landlord the full amount owing* on or before February 6, 2022, the
Tenants will start to owe interest. This
will be simple interest calculated from February 7, 2022 at 2.00% annually on
the balance outstanding.
