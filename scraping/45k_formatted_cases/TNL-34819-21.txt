King East Developments Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring Gordon J Kennedy (the 'Tenant') to pay the rent
that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by
way of video conference on January 10, 2022. Only the Landlord’s legal representative Howard
Levenson attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.      
The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
July 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021. Additional rent has come due for the month
of October 2021.
 
3.     The Tenant made no payments
since the application was filed.
 
4.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on October
31, 2021.
 
5.     The Landlord is not holding
a last month’s rent deposit.
 
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord
$6,800.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: TNL-34819-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before February
25, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 26, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
