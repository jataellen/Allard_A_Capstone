Order under Subsection 74(14)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: TEL-14412-21-VO
 
 
In the matter of:  302, 837 SIMCOE STREET
S
OSHAWA ON L1H4K8
 
Between:                  Harry Haiquan Chen                                                                                    Landlord
 
 
and
 
Mitchell Adams                                                                                                                 Tenant
 
 
 
Mitchell Adams (the 'Tenant') filed a motion to set aside order TEL-14412-21 because,
before the eviction order
was enforced, the Tenant paid the amount
required under subsection 74(11) of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the
'Act') to void the order.
 
This motion
was heard in by video conference on January 21, 2022, beginning at 9:00 a.m..
 
Only the Tenant attended the
hearing. As of 9:45 a.m., the Landlord was not present or represented at this hearing
although properly served
with a notice of the hearing by the Board.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.       
The Tenant has not previously made a motion
under subsection 74(11) of the Act to set
aside an eviction order during the period of the Tenant's tenancy agreement
with the Landlord
 
2.       
The Tenant has paid $2,023.04 to the Landlord
and this amount
is the amount required
under subsection 74(11) of the Act to void the eviction order.
 
3.       
The motion was uncontested.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.       
Order TEL-14412-21 is void and cannot
be enforced by the Landlord.
