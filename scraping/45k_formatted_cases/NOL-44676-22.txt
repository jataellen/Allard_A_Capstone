Greater Sudbury Housing
Corporation (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Jennifer
Agawa (the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant failed
to meet a condition
specified in the order issued by the Board on December 23, 2021 with respect to
application NOL-43110-21.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The order provided that the Landlord could apply to
the Board under section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act')
without notice to the Tenant
to terminate the tenancy and evict the Tenant if the Tenant
did not meet certain condition(s) specified in the
order.
 
2.     I find that the Tenant has not met the following
conditions specified in the order: On December 16, 2021 the Tenants
guest were buzzing other tenants in order to gain access to the building and go
to Jen’s apartment. On December 17, 2021 people were coming and going to the
unit from 4pm to 11pm disturbing other tenants on the floor. December 18, 2021
between 3 and 4 am the Tenant in 206 the neighbour was woken up by someone knocking
at his door. They were told to go away and continued to knock on units 207 door and the police were called.
On December 19, 2021 at 12:30 am the tenant in 206
called security regarding the volume and banging coming from the unit which
woke the Tenant. December 23, 2021 at 10:47 am the Tenant in 206 called
security regarding the volume of the music. The base from the stereo booms
through the wall. Two hours later security was notified again for the same
reason.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     Order NOL-43110-21 is cancelled.




File Number: NOL-44676-22
 
2.    
The tenancy between the Landlord and the Tenant
is terminated. The Tenant must move
out of the rental unit on or before January 31, 2022.
 
3.     If the unit is not vacated on or before
January 31, 2022, then starting
February 1, 2022, the Landlord may file this order
with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the eviction may be
enforced.
 
4.     Upon receipt of this order,
the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after February 1, 2022.
