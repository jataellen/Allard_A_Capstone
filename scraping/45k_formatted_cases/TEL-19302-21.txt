Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

In the matter
of:


8 SOUTH
MURRAY STREET QUINTE WEST
  ON K8V1Y3


 




Between:


Elaine Steiginga


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Allen Jim Madill


Tenant



 
 
Elaine Steiginga (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Allen Jim
Madill (the 'Tenant') because the Landlord requires possession of the rental
unit for the purpose of residential occupation
 
This application was heard by video conference on January 11, 2022.
 
Only the Landlord attended
the hearing. The Tenant appeared
at 1:50 p.m., after the matter had been heard.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Landlord served the Tenant
with an N12 notice of termination, alleging that she required the unit for the purpose
of residential occupation for her child. The termination date was August 31, 2021.
 
2.    
The Landlord said that she had not yet paid the compensation equal
to one month’s rent, as required pursuant to section 55.1
of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the ‘Act’).
 
3.     Consequently, I dismissed
the Landlord’s application.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Landlord's application is dismissed.
