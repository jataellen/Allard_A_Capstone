Order under
Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: CEL-97682-20
 
 



In the matter
of:


9 MORNINGMIST STREET BRAMPTON ON L6R2A5


 




Between:


Obiageli Azogu


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Harjot Singh Bains


Tenant



 
 
 
Obiageli Azogu (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring Harjot Singh Bains
(the 'Tenant') to pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on February 7, 2022. Only the Landlord attended the hearing. As of 12:25 p.m., the Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing although properly served with notice of
this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period from
May 1, 2020 to November 1, 2020.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on November
1, 2020. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent was $2,350.00.
 
4.    
The Landlord is holding
a last month's rent deposit in the amount of $2,350.00 which
will be applied against any amount determined to be outstanding.
 
5.    
The Tenant has made no payments
since the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord
$4,327.26, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to November 1, 2020 less the last month’s rent deposit.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: CEL-97682-20
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the
Landlord the full amount owing on or before March 7, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from March 8, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
