Order under Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: SOL-25458-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


1402, 192 HUGHSON STREET
N HAMILTON ON L8L7Z9


 




Between:


192 Hughson Street Inc.


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Daniel Medeiros


Tenant



 
 
 
192 Hughson
Street Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Daniel Medeiros (the 'Tenant') to pay the rent that the
Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on February 1, 2022. Only the Landlord’s Legal Representative, John Edlund, attended
the hearing. As of 2:22 p.m., the Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing
although properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
October 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant is in possession of the rental
unit.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent is $1,300.49.
 
4.     The Tenant has made no payments since
the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant
shall pay to the Landlord
$1,300.49, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant shall
also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SOL-25458-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before February
19, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 20, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
