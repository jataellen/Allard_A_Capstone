Paul Saranic (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Daiquiri-Anne Petersen (the 'Tenant')
because the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on January 24, 2022. The Landlord and the
Tenant attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period
from July 1, 2021 to January 31, 2022.
Because of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of Termination
effective September 30, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on October
15, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent was $1,533.00.
 
4.    
The Landlord is not holding a last month's
rent deposit.
 
5.     The Tenant has made no payments since the application was filed.
 
6.     The Landlord waives
the application filing
fee.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy is terminated as of October
15, 2021, the date the Tenant returned
vacant possession of the rental unit to the Landlord.
 
2.     The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord
$4,921.00*, which represents the amount of rent
owing and compensation up to October 15, 2021.




File Number: HOL-12227-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant
does not pay the Landlord
the full amount owing* on or before
February 19, 2022, the Tenant
will start to owe interest. This will
be simple interest calculated from February 20, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
