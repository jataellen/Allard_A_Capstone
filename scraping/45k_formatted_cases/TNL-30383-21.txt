Schickedanz Bros.
Properties (the 'Landlord') applied for an order
requiring Farzad Rafigh
and Parisa Homatash (the 'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 8, 2021.
Only the Landlord’s representative, S. Ursino, attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenants
did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period from
December 1, 2020 to November 30, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenants
paid $22,367.00 after the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$2,204.12, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to November 30, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: TNL-30383-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not
pay the Landlord the full amount owing on or before January 28, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from January 29,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
