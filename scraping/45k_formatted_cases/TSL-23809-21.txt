Order under Section
69
Residential Tenancies
Act, 2006
 
File Number: TSL-23809-21
 
 



In the
  matter of:


2502, 85 THORNCLIFFE PARK DRIVE TORONTO
  ON M4H1L6


 




Between:


Morguard
  Corporation


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Kemal Ata Meriam Jaiffer


Tenants



 
 
 
Morguard
Corporation (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and
evict Meriam Jaiffer and Kemal Ata (the 'Tenants') because they have been
persistently late in paying their rent. The Landlord also claimed compensation for each day the Tenants
remained in the unit
after the termination date.
 
This application was heard via videoconference on December 7, 2021.
 
Only the Landlord’s Legal Representative Faith
McGregor attended the hearing.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.   
The Landlord
applied for an order on August 23, 2021.
 
2.   
The Tenant
vacated the rental
unit as of November 1, 2021. The Tenant was in
possession at the time of the application.




File Number: TSL-23809-21
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The tenancy
between the Landlord
and the Tenants is terminated, as of November
1, 2021, the date the tenants provided vacant possession to the
Landlord.
 
2.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord the application fee of $186.00.
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount owing on or before
January 22, 2022 the Tenant
will start to owe interest. This will
be simple interest calculated from January 23, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
