Order under Section 77
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: EAL-99294-22
 
 



In the matter
of:


E, 514 LYON STREET NORTH OTTAWA ON K1R5X9


 




Between:


Mark Farrell


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Eden Neville
  Noah Cook


Tenants



 
 
 
Mark Farrell (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Eden Neville
and Noah Cook (the 'Tenants') because the Tenants gave a notice to terminate
the tenancy.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenants gave the Landlord
a notice to terminate their tenancy effective
March 31, 2022.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy between
the Landlord and the Tenants
is terminated. The Tenants must move out of the rental unit on or
before March 31, 2022.
 
2.     If the unit is not vacated on or before
March 31, 2022, then starting
April 1, 2022, the
Landlord may file this order with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so
that the eviction may be enforced.
 
3.    
Upon receipt of this order, the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after April 1, 2022.
