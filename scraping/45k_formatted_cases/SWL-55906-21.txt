2341363 Ontario Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Alexander Reyhanian
(the 'Tenant') to pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on January 27, 2022. Only the Landlord's Legal Representative, Jane Dean, attended
the hearing. As of the hearing time, the Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing
although properly served with notice of this hearing by the
Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
November 1, 2020 to January 31, 2022.
 
2.     The Tenant is in possession of the rental
unit.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent is $825.00.
 
4.     The Tenant has made no payments since
the application was filed.
 
5.    
The amount outstanding to January 31, 2022, inclusive of rent arrears
($10,875.00), and costs
($201.00), is $11,076.00.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $10,875.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to January 31, 2022.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SWL-55906-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not
pay the Landlord the full amount owing on or before February
14, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 15, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
