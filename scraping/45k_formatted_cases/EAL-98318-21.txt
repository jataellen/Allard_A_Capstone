Vince Atkinson


Tenant



 
 
Skyline Living (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring
Vince Atkinson (the 'Tenant') to pay
the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by
video conference on February
1, 2022. Only the Landlord's Legal Representative, Adelina Andreita,
attended the hearing. As of 10:53 a.m. the Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing
although properly served
with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period
from August 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on October
31, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
3.    
The lawful monthly rent was $1,359.88.
4.    
The Tenant has made no payments
since the application was filed. The amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021 is $2,656.75.
5.    
The Landlord incurred costs of $201.00
for filing the application and are is entitled to reimbursement of those costs.
6.    
The Landlord collected a last month’s
rent deposit of $1,359.88 from the Tenant.
This deposit is still
being held by the Landlord
and will be offset against
the amount owing.
 
7.     Interest on the rent deposit
has been paid to the Tenant up to February
28, 2021.
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy is terminated as of October
31, 2021, the date the Tenant gave vacant
possession of the rental unit to the Landlord.
2.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $1,296.87, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021, less the rent deposit.

3.    
The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount owing on or before
February 27, 2022, the Tenant
will start to owe interest. This will
be simple interest calculated from February 28, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
