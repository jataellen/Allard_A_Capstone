Adelaide North Developments Inc (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy
and evict Megan Braithwaite (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant failed to meet a
condition specified in the order issued by the Board on December 14, 2021 with
respect to application SWL-54036-21.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The order provided that the Landlord could apply to
the Board under section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the
'Act') without notice to the Tenant to terminate the tenancy and evict the Tenant if the Tenant
did not meet certain condition
specified in the order.
 
2.     I find that the Tenant has not met the following condition
specified in the order: The Tenant did not pay $100.00 (hydro
payment) on or before January 1, 2022.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     Order SWL-54036-21 is cancelled.
 
2.    
The tenancy between the Landlord and the Tenant
is terminated. The Tenant must move
out of the rental unit on or before February 1, 2022.
 
3.     If the unit is not vacated on or before
February 1, 2022, then starting
February 2, 2022, the Landlord may file this order
with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the eviction may be
enforced.




File Number: SWL-57502-22
 
 
 
 
4.    
Upon receipt of this order, the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after February 2, 2022.
