Order under Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: CEL-02735-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


210, 536 IVINGS DRIVE PORT ELGIN ON N0H2C1


 




Between:


Skyline Living


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Jennifer
Laforme


Tenant



 
 
 
Skyline Living (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring
Jennifer Laforme (the 'Tenant') to pay
the rent that the Tenant owes. The Landlord also claimed administration charges
related to NSF cheques.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on December 21, 2021.
Only the Landlord’s Agent, Lyla Dumford, attended the hearing. As of 12:45 p.m., the Tenant was not
present or represented at the hearing although
properly served with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
August 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
 
2.    
The Landlord
incurred charges of $20.00 for administration charges
related to cheques tendered by or on behalf of the
Tenant, which were returned NSF.
 
3.    
The Tenant vacated the rental unit on
September 30, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the
date the application was filed.
 
4.    
The lawful
monthly rent was
$1,632.65.
 
5.    
The Tenant paid
a last month’s rent deposit
of $1,632.65, which is still being
held by the Landlord. This amount will be deducted from the total amount
determined to be outstanding.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant
shall pay to the Landlord
$1,580.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to September 30, 2021 less the last month’s rent deposit.




File Number: CEL-02735-21
 
2.    
The Tenant
shall also pay to the Landlord $20.00,
which represents the total charges related to NSF cheques tendered to
the Landlord by or on behalf of the Tenant
 
3.    
The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before January
16, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from January 17, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
