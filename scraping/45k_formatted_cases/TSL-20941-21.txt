Order under Section
69
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: TSL-20941-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


111, 25 ST MARY STREET TORONTO ON M4Y1R2


 




Between:


La Place Marie


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Ronaldo
Rosales


Tenant



 
 
 
La Place Marie (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate
the tenancy and evict Ronaldo Rosales (the 'Tenant') because the
Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard via videoconference July 21, 2021 at 1:00 p.m.
 
The Landlord,
represented by David Ciobotaru, a licensed Paralegal, and the Tenant attended
the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant
has not paid the total rent the Tenant
was required to pay for the period
from February 1, 2021 to July 31, 2021. Because of the arrears,
the Landlord served a Notice of Termination effective February
26, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenant
was in possession of the rental unit when the application was
filed.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent is
$2,000.00
 
4.    
The Landlord
collected a rent deposit of $2,000.00 from the Tenant and this deposit
is still being held by the Landlord.
 
5.    
Interest on the rent deposit is owing to the Tenant for the period from November 2, 2020
to February 26, 2021.
 
6.    
The Tenant has not made any payments since the application was filed.
 
7.    
As of the hearing
date the Tenant
owed the Landlord
$12,179.13 including arrears
of rent of $11,993.13 to July
31, 2021 and the application fee of $186.00.
 
It is ordered that:




File Number: TSL-20941-21
 
1.    
The Landlords’ application for eviction
of the Tenants is denied
on the condition that:
 
a)    
The Tenant
shall pay the full lawful
monthly rent on or before the first day of each
month, commencing August 1, 2021 and for the following 9 months up to and
including May 1, 2022
 
b)    
On or before the 20th day of August 2021 the Tenant shall pay the Landlord
$6,100.00
 
c)     
Commencing September
1, 2021 and for the following 7 months ( April 1, 2022) the Tenant will pay the Landlord on or before the1st day of each month an
additional
$1,000.00 which will be applied towards
outstanding rental arrears.
 
d)    
On or before May 1, 2022 the Tenant will pay the Landlord
$179.13 as the final
payment which will satisfy all arrears contained in this order.
 
2.    
If the Tenant fail to comply
with the conditions set out in paragraph 1 above, then, within
30 days of the breach, the Landlord may apply under section 78 of the
Residential
Tenancies Act, 2006 (the ‘Act’) for an order
terminating the tenancy
and evicting the Tenants. The Landlord must make the
application within 30 days of a breach of a condition set out in paragraph 1 of
this order.
