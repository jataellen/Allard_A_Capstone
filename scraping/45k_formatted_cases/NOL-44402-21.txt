Landlord

Jorden Oliver


Tenant



 
 
 
Rodney Scully (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring Jorden Oliver (the 'Tenant') to pay
the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by video conference on February 3, 2022.
The Landlord and the Tenant attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant
was required to pay for the period
from June 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
3.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on November
1, 2021.
 
4.     The rental arrears
outstanding to October
31, 2021 total
$1,900.00.
 
5.     At the hearing,
the Tenant did admit to still owing
the outstanding rent to the Landlord.
 
6.     This order contains
all of the reasons in this matter
and no further reasons will be given.
 
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $1,900.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant shall
also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: NOL-44402-21
 
 
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount owing on or before
February 28, 2022, the Tenant
will start to owe interest. This will
be simple interest calculated from March 1, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
