Nagesh Venkatachalappa (the
'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Sheliza Bacchus
(the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes and because the Tenant, another
occupant of the rental unit or someone the Tenant permitted in the residential
complex has wilfully or negligently caused undue damage to the premises. The
Landlord has also applied for an order requiring the Tenant to compensate the
Landlord for the damage.
 
This application was heard via
videoconference on June 29, 2021 at
9:00 a.m.
 
Only the Landlord, Nagesh
Venkatachalappa, attended the hearing. The Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing
though being properly served with the Notice of Hearing by the Board.
 
The hearing proceeded without
the Tenants at 12:22 p.m.
 
 
Determinations:
 
For the L1 – rental arrears
portion of the application:
 
1.    
The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
November 1, 2020 to June 30, 2021. Because
of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of Termination effective November
18, 2020.
 
2.     The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit when the application was
filed.
 
3.     The Landlord collected a rent deposit
of $1,895.00 from the Tenant
and this deposit
is still being held by the
Landlord.




File Number: TEL-14813-21
 
4.    
Interest on the rent deposit is owing to the Tenant for the period from August 2, 2020 to November 18, 2020.
 
5.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on May 30, 2021
 
 
For the L2 – damages to the
rental unit portion of the application:
 
 
6.    
The Landlord did not submit any supporting document.
 
It is ordered that:
 
For the L1 – rental arrears
portion of the application:
 
1.    
The tenancy between the Landlord
and the Tenant is terminated May 30, 2021, the day the Tenant moved out of the rental unit and returned
vacant possession to the Landlord.
 
2.     The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord
$10,737.87*, which represents the amount of rent
owing and compensation up to May 30, 2021, less the rent deposit and interest
the Landlord owes on the rent deposit.
 
3.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.     If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord the full amount
owing* on or before
January 19, 2022, the Tenant will
start to owe interest. This will be
simple interest calculated from January 30, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
 
For the L2 – damages to the
rental unit portion of the application:
 
5.    
The application is dismissed.
