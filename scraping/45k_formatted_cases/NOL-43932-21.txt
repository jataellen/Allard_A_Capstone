Skyline Living
(the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring William Stamm
(the 'Tenant') to pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
The Landlord also claimed administration charges related to NSF
cheques.
 
This application was heard by videoconference
on January 13, 2022.
 
Only the
Landlord's Legal Representative, Kayleigh Maille, attended the hearing. As of 11:32 a.m., the Tenant was not present
or represented at the hearing
although properly served
with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant did not pay the total rent
the Tenant was required to pay for the period from
February 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on
September 30, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the
date the application was filed.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent was $1,147.96.
 
4.    
The Landlord incurred charges
of $60.00 for administration charges
related to cheques tendered by or on behalf of the
Tenant, which were returned NSF.
 
5.     The Landlord is not holding
a last month's rent deposit.
 
6.     The Tenant
has made no payments since
the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:




File Number: NOL-43932-21
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $2,300.51, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to September 30, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $60.00,
which represents the total charges related to NSF cheques tendered to
the Landlord by or on behalf of the Tenant
 
3.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.     If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord the full amount
owing on or before January
31, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 1, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
