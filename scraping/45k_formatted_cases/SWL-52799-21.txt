Order under
Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies
Act, 2006
File Number: SWL-52799-21
 



In the
  matter of:


408, 500 SPRINGBANK DR LONDON ON N6J4G6


 




Between:


500 Springbank Drive Limited


Landlord




 


and


 




 


Charlotte Hele
  James Hele


Tenants



 
500 Springbank Drive Limited
(the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring James Hele and Charlotte Hele (the 'Tenants') to pay
the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on January 13, 2022. Only the Landlord’s Legal Representative,
David Ciobutaru, attended the hearing. As of 9:54 a.m., the Tenants were not
present or represented at the hearing although
properly served with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenants were in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenants vacated
the rental unit on July 31, 2021.
 
3.     The last month's
rent deposit was applied to the rent for July
2021.
 
4.    
The Tenants did not pay the total rent they were required to pay for the period
from March 1, 2021 to June 30, 2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord $5,260.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to June 30, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00 for the cost of filing
the application.
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not pay the
Landlord the full amount owing on or before February 1, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest.
This will be simple interest
calculated from February 2,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
