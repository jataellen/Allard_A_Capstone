Order under Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: TNL-34331-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


161 FINCH AVE W TORONTO ON M2N2J1


 




Between:


Daniela Cristini


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Francesca Groves Nigel Gowers


Tenants



 
 
 
Daniela Cristini
(the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Nigel Gowers
and Francesca Groves (the
'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application
was heard by videoconference on December 6, 2021. Only the Only the Landlord's Agent, Bryan Rubin attended the
hearing. As of 10:37 a.m., the
Tenants were not present or represented at the hearing
although properly served
with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants
did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period from May 1, 2021 to August 31, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenants vacated the rental unit on
August 31, 2021. The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent was $ 3,750.00
 
4.    
The Landlord
is not holding a last month's rent deposit.
 
5.     The Tenants have made no payments since the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on August 31, 2021.
The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$13,250.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to August 31, 2021.




File Number: TNL-34331-21
 
3.    
The Tenants
shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord the
full amount owing on or before February 4, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from February 5,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
