Cannifton Road Investments Ltd (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring Hannah Hutchins (the
'Tenant') to pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on February 2, 2022. Only the Landlord's Legal Representative, Ken Sit, attended the hearing. As of 11:12 a.m., the Tenant was not present
or represented at the hearing although properly served with notice of
this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on
September 30, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the
date the application was filed.
 
2.     The lawful monthly
rent is $899.94
 
3.     The Tenant made no payments
after the application was filed.
 
4.    
The Landlord collected a rent deposit
of $899.94 from the Tenant and this deposit
will be applied towards the
outstanding arrears.
 
5.    
Interest on the rent deposit has been paid to the Tenant for the period from April
1, 2019 to March 31, 2021.
 
6.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
August 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021, in the total amount of $1,799.88.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on
September 30, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the
date the application was filed.




File Number: TEL-20102-21
 
2.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $899.94,
which represents the amount of rent owing up to September 30, 2021, less the
rent deposit.
 
3.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.     If the Tenant does
not pay the Landlord the full amount
owing on or before February
25, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 26, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
