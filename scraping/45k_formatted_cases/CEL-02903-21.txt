Darrel Tayloo (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Anita D'souza (the 'Tenant') because the
Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on January 10, 2022. Only the Landlord
attended the hearing. As of 11:03 a.m., the Tenant was not present
or represented at the hearing
although properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period from
July 1, 2021 to January 31, 2022. Because
of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of Termination effective July 21,
2021.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on
October 31, 2021. The Tenant
was in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was
filed.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent was $1,350.00.
 
4.    
The Landlord is not holding a last month's
rent deposit.
 
5.    
The Tenant has made no payments
since the application was filed.
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on
October 31, 2021. The Tenant
was in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was
filed.
 
2.     The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord
$5,400.00*, which represents the amount of rent
owing and compensation up to October 31, 2021.




File Number: CEL-02903-21
 
3.    
The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.     If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord the full amount
owing* on or before February
20, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 21, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
