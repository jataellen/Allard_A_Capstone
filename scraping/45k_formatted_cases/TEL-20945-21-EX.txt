On November 8,
2021, Danbar Property Holdings Ii Lp (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to
terminate the tenancy and evict Carl Noseworthly and Robin Bowmen (the 'Tenants') because
the Tenants failed to meet a condition specified in the order issued by
the Board on November 3, 2021 with respect to application TEL-17846-21.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The order provided that
the Landlord could apply to the Board under section 78 of the Residential
Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act') without notice to the Tenants to terminate
the tenancy and evict
the Tenants if the Tenants
did not meet certain condition(s) specified in the order.
 
2.    
I find that the Tenants have not met the following
condition specified in the order:
 
The Tenants
or occupants or guests of the rental
unit were not to engage in the conduct
noted in the N5 Notice of Termination which led to the Landlord’s application. This included, but not limited to loud
banging, stomping or yelling.
 
On October 29,
2021, beginning at approximately 7:30 am and continuing until approximately 8:00 am, excessive noise consisting of loud stomping
and banging was emanating from the Tenants’
residential unit.
 
On November 3,
2021, beginning at approximately 12:00 am and continuing until approximately 12:30 am, again
beginning at approximately 5:00 am and continuing until approximately 5:30 am, excessive
noise consisting of loud stomping and banging was emanating from the Tenants’
residential unit. On the same day,
beginning at approximately 8:40 am and continuing until approximately 9:15 am,
excessive noise consisting of loud stomping and banging was emanating from the
Tenants’ residential unit.
 
On November 4,
2021, beginning at approximately 12:30 am and continuing until approximately 2:00 am, excessive noise consisting of loud stomping
and excessive dragging was emanating from the Tenants’ residential unit.




File Number: TEL-20945-21-EX
 
On November 5,
2021, beginning at approximately 7:45 am and continuing until approximately 9:30 am, and then starting
at approximately 10:00 am and continuing until approximately 11:15 am, excessive
noise consisting of loud stomping, excessive dragging and yelling was emanating
from the Tenants’ residential unit.
 
And on November 6, 2021, beginning at approximately
7:00 am and continuing until approximately 9:45 am, excessive
noise consisting of loud stomping, banging and slamming of doors was emanating
from the Tenants’ residential unit.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
Order TEL-17846-21 is cancelled.
 
2.    
The tenancy
between the Landlord
and the Tenants
is terminated. The Tenants must move out of the rental unit on or
before January 15, 2022.
 
3.    
If the unit is not vacated
on or before January 15, 2022, then starting January
16, 2022, the Landlord may
file this order with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the
eviction may be enforced.
 
4.    
Upon receipt
of this order,
the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after January 16, 2022.
