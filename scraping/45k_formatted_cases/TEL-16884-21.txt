Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

In the matter
of:


MAIN FLOOR, 1665
AVONMORE SQUARE PICKERING
  ON L1V7H2


 




Between:


Veena Joshi


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Kevin Matherson
  Tracy Dittmer


Tenants



 
 
 
Veena Joshi (the 'Landlord')
applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Kevin Matherson and Tracy Dittmer
(the 'Tenants') because
they have been persistently late in paying their rent.
 
The Landlord also claimed
compensation for each day the Tenants remained
in the unit after the termination date.
 
This application was heard via videoconference on August 17, 2021.
The Landlord and the Tenants attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.    The Tenant have been persistently late in pay their rent.
 
2.   
The Landlord submitted that the Tenants
have not paid the rent when it is due and payable each and every month since March
2021, the Tenants did not dispute this submission.
 
3.    The Tenants were in possession of the rental
unit when the application was filed.
 
4.    The tenancy agreement
between the Landlord
and Tenants began
August 2017.
 
5.    The lawful monthly
rent is $2,145.00
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Landlord’s application to terminate tenancy
and evict the Tenants based
on the following conditions:




File Number: TEL-16884-21
 
a)     Commencing September 1, 2021 and for
the subsequent 11 months, up to and including
August 1, 2022 the Tenants
shall on or before the 1st day of each month pay the Landlord the full lawful monthly
rent.
 
b)    
The Tenants shall pay the Landlord
the application filing fee of $186.00 on or before January 31, 2022.
 
2.    
If the Tenants fails to make any
one of the payments in accordance with this order, the outstanding balance of
any arrears of rent and costs to be paid by the Tenants to the Landlord pursuant
to paragraph 1 of this order shall become immediately due and owing and the Landlord may, without
notice to the Tenants apply to the Board pursuant to section 78 of the Act for
an order terminating the tenancy and evicting the Tenants and requiring that
the Tenants pay any new arrears, NSF fees and related charges that became owing
after August 17, 2021
