Capreit Limited Partnership (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict
Maureen O'Loughlin and Julia Guyatt
(the 'Tenants') because
the Tenants did not pay the rent that
the Tenants owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on December 21, 2021. The Landlord's Legal Representative, Jason
Paine and the Tenants attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant is in possession of the rental
unit.
 
2.     The monthly rent is $1,926.09.
 
3.     The Tenants paid $9,630.45 in arrears for the period
ending December 31, 2021.
 
4.    
The only amount outstanding is the Landlord's cost of filing
the application. As a result,
the order will be limited to costs only and not eviction.
