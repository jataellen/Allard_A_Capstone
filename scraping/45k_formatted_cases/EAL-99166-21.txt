Arkadiusz
Arkowski and Kinga Arkowska (the 'Landlords') applied for an order to terminate
the tenancy and evict Emilie Westbrook (the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant entered
into an agreement to terminate the tenancy.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    The Landlords
and the Tenant signed an agreement to terminate the tenancy as of
February 1, 2022.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The tenancy
between the Landlords
and the Tenant is terminated. The Tenant must move out of the rental unit on or
before February 1, 2022.
 
2.    
If the unit is not vacated
on or before February 1, 2022, then starting February
2, 2022, the Landlords
may file this order with the
Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that
the eviction may be enforced.
 
3.    
Upon receipt of this order,
the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlords on or after February 2, 2022.
