Felcity Meloche Jeff Meloche

Brighton Court Apartments (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Fellcity
Meloche and Jeff Meloche (the 'Tenants') to pay the
rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on January 27, 2022. Only the Landlord’s Agent, Carol Prior, and the Landlord's Legal Representative,
Bryan Rubin, attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants did not pay the total rent they were required to pay for the period from
September 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenants vacated the rental unit on September 30, 2021. The Tenants were in
possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent is $1,500.00.
 
4.     The Tenants made no payments
after the application was filed.
 
5.     The Landlord is not holding
a last months rent deposit.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord $1,500.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to September 30, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SWL-55773-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before February
14, 2022, the Tenants
will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from February 15,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
