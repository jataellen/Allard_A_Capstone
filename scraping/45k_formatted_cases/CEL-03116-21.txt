  matter of:

Between:


Rajeev Manmatharajan


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Erin Michelle Jeremy Dearing


Tenants



 
 
Rajeev Manmatharajan (the 'Landlord') applied for an order determining that Erin Michelle
and Jeremy Dearing (the 'Tenants') abandoned the rental unit
 
This application was heard in Passcode: 956 0593 7653# on January
27, 2022. Only the
Landlord attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Landlord discovered that the
Tenants may have abandoned the rental unit is September 2021. The Landlord
contacted the Tenant and was told he could apply their
last month rent deposit to September rent.
 
2.     The Tenants did not remove
their belongings from the
unit until November
30, 2021.
 
3.     I find the Tenants abandoned
the rental unit.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy was terminated on November 30,
2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord
$186.00 for the cost of filing the application.
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not pay the
Landlord the full amount owing on or before February 11, 2022, they will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from February 12, 2022 at 2.00% annually on
the balance outstanding.
