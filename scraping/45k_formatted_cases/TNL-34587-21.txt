Victoria Macdonald (the
'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy of Brian Lampron, Ross Macdonald and Allan Lampron
(the 'Sub Tenants'), and for compensation for the use of the
rental unit.
 
This application was heard via videoconference on December 14, 2021.
The Landlord and the Sub Tenants attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.     The Landlord filed
this application with the Board
on June 22, 2021.
 
2.     The Landlord stated that she had attempted to file the
application on April 9, 2021, however that application had been rejected
by the Board. She was unable to provide an explanation as to why it then took
until June 22, 2021 to file this application.
 
3.     The Landlord confirmed
that she became aware of the presence
of the sub-tenants on March
2, 2021 as set out in the application.
 
4.    
Section 104(4)(c) provides that an assignment of the tenancy
is a deemed assignment if the Landlord does not apply within 60
days for an order evicting the sub-tenants.
 
5.     The Landlord did not perfect
an application within the 60 days provided
for in the Act and as a result, the application cannot
proceed.




File Number: TNL-34587-21
 
 
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Landlord’s application is dismissed.
