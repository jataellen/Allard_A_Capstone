Centurion Property Associates Inc (the 'Landlord')
applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Atiq Ur Rehman (the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant failed to meet a condition specified in the order issued
by the Board on November 25, 2019 with respect to application CEL-89532- 19.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The order provided that the Landlord could apply to
the Board under section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act') without notice
to the Tenant to terminate
the tenancy and evict the
Tenant if the Tenant did not
meet certain condition(s) specified
in the order.
 
2.     I find that the Tenant
has not met the following
condition specified in the order:
 
For The duration of the tenancy,
the Tenant and any of the rental unit occupants or permitted guests, were not
to make undue or excessive noise that has been claimed under the N5 notice,
including but not limited to loud TV volume, screaming, yelling, jumping, slamming
of doors, riding
scooters, bouncing of balls,
or any other undue or excessive noise, at any time between the hours of 10:00
pm and 8:00 am.
 
On January 9, 2022, between approximately 10pm and 11pm, sounds of stomping
and objects being thrown and dragged were emanating from the Tenant’s rental unit.
 
On January 10, 2022,
between approximately 11pm and 12am, sounds of stomping
were emanating from the Tenant’s rental unit.
 
On January 11, 2022, between
approximately 9pm and 12am, sounds of doors being slammed, objects being dropped, yelling
and stomping were emanating from the Tenant’s rental unit.




 
On January 12, 2022, between
approximately 8pm and 11pm, sounds of objects being dragged, objects being thrown, doors
being slammed, and stomping were emanating from the Tenant’s rental
unit.
 
On January 13, 2022, between
approximately 10pm and 12am, sounds of doors being slammed, objects being dropped, objects
being thrown, stomping
and yelling were emanating
from the Tenant’s rental unit.
 
On January 14, 2022, between
approximately 8pm and 12am, sounds of objects being dropped, objects being
thrown, fighting, screaming, and stomping were emanating from the Tenant’s
rental unit. At 11:34pm, the Landlord’s staff attended
at the rental and could hear loud screaming and fighting emanating from the
Tenant’s rental unit.
 
On January 15, 2022, between
approximately 9pm and 1am, sounds of doors slamming, objects being dropped
and dragged, and stomping were emanating from the Tenant’s rental unit.
 
And on January 16, 2022, between
approximately 10pm and 1am, sounds of doors slamming, yelling, and stomping were
emanating from the Tenant’s rental unit.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     Order CEL-89532-19 is cancelled.
 
2.     The tenancy between
the Landlord and the Tenant
is terminated. The Tenant must move
out of the rental unit on or before March 6, 2022.
 
3.    
If the unit is not vacated
on or before March 6, 2022, then starting March
7, 2022, the Landlord may file this order with the
Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the eviction may be enforced.
 
4.    
Upon receipt of this order, the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after March 7, 2022.
