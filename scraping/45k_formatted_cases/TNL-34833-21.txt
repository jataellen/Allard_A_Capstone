IMH POOL XX (the
'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Nabila
Malik and Waqas Mahmood (the 'Tenants') because
the Tenants did not pay the rent that the Tenants owe. The Landlord also
claimed NSF cheque charges and related administration charges.
 
This application is a duplicate
application to file number TNL-34844-21. As a result,
this application is dismissed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    The Landlord's application is dismissed.
