87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

309, 124 BROADWAY AVENUE TORONTO ON M4P1V8


 




Between:


Capreit Limited Partnership


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Roberto
Tabaniag


Tenant



 
 
 
Capreit Limited
Partnership (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and
evict Roberto Tabaniag (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant
did not pay the rent that the Tenant
owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 8, 2021.
Only the Landlord’s representative, J. Paine, attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.      
The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenant has not paid the total rent the
Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
June 1, 2021 to November 30, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenant
paid $4,661.00 after
the application was filed.
The payment covered all the arrears up to November 30, 2021, and a
portion of the application fee.
 
4.    
The Tenant
owes $69.75, which
represents the balance
of the cost of filing the application.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord
$69.75.00 for the balance of the cost of filing the
application.




File Number: TNL-33916-21
 
2.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before January
18, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from January 19, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
