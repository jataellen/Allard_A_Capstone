Brookmill Garden Homes/manor Park Manag (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate
the tenancy and evict Nolasco Dela Pena (the 'Tenant') because the
Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard via
videoconference on June 8, 2021 at 1:00
p.m.
 
Only the
Landlord, represented by Nelson Lamoureux, an employee, attended the hearing.
The Tenant, Nolascp Dela Pena, was not present
or represented at the hearing though being properly
served with the Notice of Hearing by the Board.
The hearing proceeded without
the Tenants at 4:11 p.m.
 
 
It is determined that:
 
1.    
The Tenant has not paid $2,295.00
of the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period from February 1, 2020 to
November 30, 2020. Because of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of
Termination.
 
2.     The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit when the application was
filed.
 
3.     The lawful rent monthly rent is $1,165.00
 
4.     The Landlord holds a last month’s rent deposit of
$1,095.00
 
5.     After the application was filed the Tenant
paid $9,471.00.




File Number: EAL-92584-20
 
6.    
As of the hearing
date the Tenant
owed the Landlord
$1,165.00 including arrears
of rent t June 30, 2021 and the application fee of
$186.00
 
Although the Tenant was not present
at the hearing, the Landlord
consented to the following
repayment agreement.
 
It
is ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenant shall pay the Landlord $1,165.00 as follows:
 
a)    
on or before July 15, 2021 the Tenant
shall pay the Landlord the sum
of $400.00 to be applied to
outstanding rental arrears.
 
b)    
 on or before August 15, 2021 the Tenant
shall pay the Landlord the sum
of $400.00 to be applied to
outstanding rental arrears
 
c)     
on or before September
15, 2021 the Tenant shall pay the Landlord the sum of
$365.00 in full and final settlement of arrears claimed
in this application.
 
2.     The
Tenant shall also pay the Landlord rent owing for the month of
July 1, 2021 to September 1,
2021, no later than 1st day of each month.
 
3.    
If the Tenant fails to make any
one of the payments in accordance with this order, the outstanding balance of
any arrears of rent and costs to be paid by the Tenant to the Landlord pursuant
to paragraph 1 of this order shall become immediately due and owing and the Landlord may, without notice
to the Tenant apply to the Board pursuant to section
78 of the Act for an order terminating the tenancy and evicting the Tenant and
requiring that the Tenant pay any new arrears, NSF fees and related charges
that became owing after June 8, 2021
