Order under Section
87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

In the matter
of:


27 CROWLAND AVENUE WELLAND ON L3B1W8


 




Between:


Rafay Rehman


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Aaron
Gilbert


Tenant



 
 
 
Rafay Rehman
(the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Aaron Gilbert (the 'Tenant') because the
Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on January 7, 2022.
Only the Landlord’s representative, A. Hamdani, attended
the hearing. Determinations:
1.     The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on August 9, 2021
 
3.    
The Tenant has not paid the total rent the
Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
October 1, 2020 to August 9, 2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant
shall pay to the Landlord
$14,920.74, which represents the amount of rent
owing and compensation up to August 9, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SOL-18060-20
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before January
22, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from January 23, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
