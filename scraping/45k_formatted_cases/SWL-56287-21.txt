Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

Between:                  Galaxy Value Add Ontario

and
 




Heather Willems Lemuel King


Tenants




 
 
 
Galaxy Value Add Ontario
Properties LP (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Lemuel King and Heather Willems (the
'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference
on February 3, 2022.
 
Only the Landlord's Legal
Representative, Sara Mathew, attended the hearing. As of 3:40 p.m., the Tenants
were not present
or represented at the hearing
although properly served
with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants did not pay the total rent they were required to pay for the period from
October 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants were in
possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.     The Tenants have made no payments since
the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord $946.51,
which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SWL-56287-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before March 7, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe
interest. This will be simple
interest calculated from March 8, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance
outstanding.
