Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies
Act, 2006
 
File Number: TEL-20139-21
 
 



In the
  matter of:


2 TRIPP AVENUE BELLEVILLE ON K8P4N5


 




Between:


Hastings Local
Housing Corporation


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Melissa Stone


Tenant



 
 
 
Hastings Local Housing Corporation (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate
the tenancy and evict Melissa Stone (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant, another occupant
of the rental unit or someone
the Tenant permitted in the residential complex has substantially interfered
with the reasonable enjoyment or lawful right, privilege or interest of the
Landlord or another tenant.
 
This application was heard in Passcode: 956 0593 7653#
on February 1, 2022. Only the Andrea Haggerty attended the hearing.
 
At the hearing, the Landlord’s representative consented to the following order.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenant shall obtain content
insurance, satisfactory to the Landlord’s requirements, on or before February 28, 2022.
 
2.    
In the event the Tenant fails to comply with the
condition as set out above, the
Landlord shall be entitled
to apply to the Board, no later than 30 days after a breach of this Order,
without notice to the Tenant, for an Order terminating the tenancy and evicting
the Tenant, pursuant to Section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006.
 
3.     The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord
$186.00 for the cost of the application.
 
4.     The Landlord has the right
to collect the full amount
at any time.
 
5.    
If the Tenant does not
pay the full amount owing by February 28, 2022 the Tenant will owe interest.
This will be simple interest
calculated at 2% annually on the outstanding balance.
