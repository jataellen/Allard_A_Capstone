Toronto Community Housing Corporation (the 'Landlord') applied for
an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Georges Sarkis (the 'Tenant')
because the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on December 21, 2021. Only the Landlord’s Agent, Dale Maingot, attended
the hearing. As of
10:20 a.m., the Tenant was not present
or represented at the hearing
although properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant has not paid
the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period from May 1, 2020 to December 31, 2021. Because
of the arrears, the Landlord
served a Notice of Termination effective June 18,
2021.
 
2.    
The Tenant
vacated the rental unit on September 30, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent was
$3,938.00
 
4.    
The Tenant
has made no payments since
the application was filed.
 
5.    
The Landlord
collected a rent deposit
of $3,800.00 from the Tenant
and this deposit
is still being held by the
Landlord.
 
6.    
Interest on the rent deposit has been paid to the Tenant for the period
from January 1, 2020
to December 31, 2020.
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.    
The tenancy is
terminated as of September 30, 2021, the date the Tenant gave vacant possession
of the rental unit to the Landlord.




File Number: TSL-24097-21
 
2.    
The Tenant shall pay to
the Landlord $22,248.17*, which represents the amount of rent owing and
compensation up to September 30, 2021, less the rent deposit.
 
3.    
The Tenant
shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord the
full amount owing* on or before January 25, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe
interest. This will be simple
interest calculated from January 26, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance
outstanding.
