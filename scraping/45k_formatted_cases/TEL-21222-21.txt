Taunton
Co-tenancy Limited C/o Real Star Management Partnership (the 'Landlord')
applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Barry Neads and Roberta
Bradley (the 'Tenants') because the Tenants
failed to meet a condition specified in the order issued by the Board on May
5, 2021 with respect to application TEL-10191-20.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The order provided that the Landlord could
apply to the Board under section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the
'Act') without notice to the Tenants to terminate the tenancy and evict the Tenants if the Tenants
did not meet certain condition(s) specified in the order.
 
2.    
I find that the Tenants have not met the following condition specified in the order: The Tenant failed to pay the lawful
monthly rent in full, on or before December 1, 2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The tenancy
between the Landlord
and the Tenants
is terminated. The Tenants must move out of the rental unit on or
before January 25, 2022.
 
2.    
If the unit is not vacated
on or before January 25, 2022, then starting January
26, 2022, the Landlord may
file this order with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the
eviction may be enforced.
 
3.    
Upon receipt
of this order, the Court
Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after January 26, 2022.
