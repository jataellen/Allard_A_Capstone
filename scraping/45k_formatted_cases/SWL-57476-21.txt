Canadian Mental
Health Association (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate
the tenancy and evict Shelia
McMorrow (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant entered into an agreement to
terminate the tenancy.
 
Determinations:
 
1.   
The Landlord
filed an L3 application on December 22, 2021 which notes a termination date of November 30, 2021.
 
2.   
The Landlord’s Legal Representative submitted
a declaration which noted that the Tenant was rehoused in a supportive
housing facility effective December 1, 2021.
 
3.   
As the tenancy has already terminated, the Landlord’s application seeking termination of tenancy is moot and therefore will not
be considered.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    The Landlord's application is dismissed.
