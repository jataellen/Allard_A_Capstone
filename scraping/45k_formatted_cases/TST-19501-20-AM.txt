Se Youn Kim and Philip Cho (the
'Tenants') applied for an order determining that Morguard Corporation (the
'Landlord') substantially interfered with the reasonable enjoyment of the rental
unit or residential complex by the Tenants
or by a member of their household.
 
This application was heard by way of videoconference on November 30, 2021.
 
Philip Cho (Tenant), Zeeshan
Rahman (Tenant Representative), Morguard Corporation
(the 'Landlord') and Martin Zarnett (Landlord’s Representative) attended the
hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     At the hearing,
the Tenants requested
the consent of the Board to withdraw their application on a with
prejudice basis.
 
2.     In accordance with subsection 200(4)
of the Residential Tenancies
Act, 2006, I consent to the withdrawal of this
application.
It is ordered that:




1.    
The matter having been withdrawn with prejudice, the Board’s
file is now closed.
