Between:


Metcap Living Management Inc


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Laurie
Dadswell Rick Hurst


Tenants



 
 
 
Metcap Living
Management Inc (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Rick Hurst and Laurie Dadswell
(the 'Tenants') because the Tenants did not pay the rent that the Tenants owe.
 
This application was heard via videoconference on July 21, 2021
at 9:00 a.m.
 
The Landlord,
represented by Katrina
Murrell, a licensed
Paralegal, and the Tenant, Rick Hurst
attended the hearing. Tenant Laurie Dadswell was unable to attend the hearing
but was represented by Tenant Rick Hurst.
 
It is determined
that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
have not paid $2,265.73 of the total rent the Tenants were required to pay
for the period from January 1, 2020 to March 31, 2021. Because of the arrears,
the Landlord served a Notice of Termination.
 
2.    
The Tenants
were in possession of the rental
unit when the application
was filed.
 
3.    
The monthly
rent is $1,458.44
 
4.    
As of the hearing date,
the Tenant owed the Landlord $2,427.32 including arrears of rent to July 31, 2021 of $2,241.32 and the application filing
fee of $186.00. The Tenant
agreed with the amount of rental arrears outstanding.
 
 
It is ordered that:




File Number: TSL-20802-21
 
1.    
The Tenants
shall pay the Landlord $2,451.73 as follows:
 
a)    
Commencing August
1, 2021 and until April 1, 2022, on or before the first day of
each month the Tenants shall pay the Landlord the full lawful monthly rent.
 
b)    
Commencing August 1,
2021 and until March 1, 2022 on or before the first day of each month the Tenants shall
pay the Landlord
an additional $300.00
that will be applied towards
current outstanding rental arrears
 
c)     
On or before April 1, 2022, the Tenant
will pay the Landlord an additional $27.32 towards outstanding rental arrears
which would satisfy this order.
 
2.    
If the Tenants fails to
make any one of the payments in accordance with this order, the outstanding
balance of any arrears of rent and costs to be paid by the Tenants to the
Landlord pursuant to paragraph 1 of this order shall become immediately due and owing and the Landlord may, without
notice to the Tenants apply to the Board pursuant to section 78 of the Act for
an order terminating the tenancy and evicting the Tenants and requiring that
the Tenants pay any new arrears, NSF fees and related charges that became owing
after July 21, 2021
