Order under Section 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: TSL-23857-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


121, 1401 DUPONT STREET TORONTO ON M6H2B1


 




Between:


Dupont/Lansdowne
Holdings Inc.


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Elsa Gebremichael


Tenant



 
 
 
Dupont/Lansdowne Holdings
Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy
and evict Elsa Gebremichael (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant did not pay the
rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard
by videoconference on December
15, 2021. The Landlord’s Legal Representative, Bryan Rubin and the
Tenant attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period
from August 1, 2021 to December 31, 2021. Because of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of
Termination effective August 19, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant is in possession of the rental
unit.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent is $1,300.00.
 
4.    
The Landlord
collected a rent deposit of $1,300.00 from the
Tenant and this deposit is still
being held by the Landlord.
 
5.    
Interest on the rent deposit is owing to the Tenant for the period from February 1, 2021 to August 19, 2021.
 
6.     The Tenant paid $4,700.00 after the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant
shall pay to the Landlord
$1,300.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to December 31, 2021.




File Number: TSL-23857-21
 
2.    
The Tenant
shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing
the application.
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before January 4, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest.
This will be simple interest
calculated from January 5,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
