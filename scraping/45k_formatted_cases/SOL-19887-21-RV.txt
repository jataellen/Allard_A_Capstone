Review Order
 
Yonghui Lei (the
'Landlord') applied for an order applied for an order to terminate the tenancy
John Hewak (the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant has abandoned the rental unit, and requiring
the Tenant to pay compensation for the damages caused by the Tenant or a
person the Tenant permitted in the residential complex
 
This application was resolved by order SOL-19887-21 issued on August
10, 2021. On August 16, 2021,
the Landlord requested a review of the order.
The request was heard by videoconference on December 7, 2021.
Only the Landlord attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.     The Landlord did not have a reasonable opportunity to participate in the proceedings.
 
2.     The Tenant abandoned the rental unit.
 
3.    
The Landlord’s claim for compensation for damage is denied because
the Tenant was not
possession of the rental unit at the time the application was filed.
 
It is ordered
that:
 
1.    
Order SOL-19887-21 issued on August
10, 2021 is cancelled and replaced by the
following:
 
2.     The Tenant abandoned
the rental unit.




File Number: SOL-19887-21
 
 
 
3.     The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord
$186.00 for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before January
22, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from January 23, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
