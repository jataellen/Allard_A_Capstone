Madan (the 'Landlords') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Michelle Raqueno and Khaled Iwamura
(the 'Tenants') because
the Tenants gave a notice
to terminate the tenancy.
 
This
application was heard by video conference on January 31, 2022. Only the
Landlords’ Legal Representative, B. Lockwood attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    The Tenants vacated the rental unit on
January 4, 2022. The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date the
application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    The
tenancy is terminated as of January
4, 2022, the date the Tenants gave vacant
possession of the rental unit to the Landlord.
