Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

In the matter
of:


2, 130 ECCLES
STREET OTTAWA ON K1R6S8


 




Between:


Thuan Hang Dung My Hang


Landlords




 


 
and


 




 


Devin Huff


Tenant



 
 
 
Thuan Hang and Dung My Hang (the 'Landlords') applied for an order
to terminate the tenancy
and evict Devin Huff (the 'Tenant')
 
This application was heard in Passcode:
956 0593 7653#
on January 26, 2022.
 
Only the Landlord, Thuan Hang attended
the hearing. Tony Hang attended as a translator for the Landlord.
 
Determinations:
 
 
1.    The termination date on the second N5
Notice is October 9, 2021 and it was served on October 4, 2021. The termination date on the second N5 Notice must be at least 14 days
from the date it’s served pursuant to section 68(1) (2) of the Residential
Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act'). The Landlord gave only 5 days which
renders the N5 Notice void.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    The Landlord’s application is dismissed.
