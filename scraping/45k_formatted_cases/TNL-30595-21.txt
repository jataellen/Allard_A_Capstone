Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

28
  TAYROW ROAD ETOBICOKE ON M9W2T9


 




Between:


Shilpy Sharma


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Bradley James Kennedy Suman Kennedy


Tenants



 
 
 
Shilpy Sharma (the 'Landlord') applied for an order
requiring Suman Kennedy
and Bradley James Kennedy (the 'Tenants') to pay the
rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 8, 2021.
The Landlord and the Tenants attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenants vacated
the rental unit on March 1, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenants did not pay the total rent they were required to pay for the period from
December 1, 2019 to March 1, 2021.
 
4.    
The rent was originally $2,300.00
a month. Effective December 6, 2019, the Landlord agreed to reduce the rent to $2,200.00 a month in exchange for the Tenants
assuming the cost of utilities.
 
5.    
The Landlord claims the Tenant failed
to pay the cost of utilities. The Landlord may file a separate application to seek
compensation for unpaid utilities.
 
6.     The Tenants have been credited
with a rent deposit in the amount
of paid $2,300.
 
7.     The Tenants failed to establish that they paid the Landlord a rent deposit
in the amount of
$650.00.




File Number: TNL-30595-21
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord $3,474.15, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to March 1, 2021, less the rent deposit.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing
the application.
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not pay the
Landlord the full amount owing on or before February 4, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from February 5,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
