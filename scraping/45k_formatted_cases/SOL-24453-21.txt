Order under Section 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: SOL-24453-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


12, 1050 HIGHLAND STREET BURLINGTON ON L7R3T9


 




Between:


Equiton Residential Income Fund  Lp


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Russell Palk


Tenant



 
 
 
Equiton Residential Income Fund Lp (the 'Landlord') applied for an order
to terminate the tenancy and evict Russell Palk (the 'Tenant')
because the Tenant did not pay the rent that the
Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 23, 2021.
Only the Landlord’s representative, R. Forster, attended
the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on September 30, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
June 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the
Landlord $1,968.90*, which represents the amount of rent owing and compensation up to
September 30, 2021, less the rent deposit
and interest the Landlord owes on the rent deposit.
 
2.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SOL-24453-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before February
12, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 13, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
