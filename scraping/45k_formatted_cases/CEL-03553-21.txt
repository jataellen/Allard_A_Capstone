apreit Limited Partnership (the
'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Clement
McNeil Fullington (the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on February 17, 2022. Only the Landlord’s Legal Representative, Jason
Paine, attended the hearing. As of 10:22 a.m.,
the Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing
although properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period
from October 1, 2021 to February 28, 2022. Because of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of Termination
effective October 21, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenant vacated the rental unit on
February 10, 2022. The Tenant was in possession
of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent was $1,690.00.
 
4.     The Tenant has made no payments since
the application was filed.
 
5.    
The Landlord
collected a rent deposit of $1,690.00 from the Tenant and this deposit is still
being held by the Landlord.
 
6.    
Interest on the rent deposit is owing to the Tenant for the period from July 15, 2021 to October 21, 2021.
 
It is ordered
that:
 
1.    
The tenancy
is terminated as of February
10, 2022 the date the Tenant returned
vacant possession of the rental unit to the Landlord.




File Number: CEL-03553-21
 
2.    
The Tenant shall pay to the
Landlord $5,699.05*, which represents the amount of rent owing and compensation up to February
10, 2022, less the rent deposit and interest the Landlord owes on the rent deposit.
 
3.     The Tenant shall
also pay to the Landlord
$186.00 for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.     If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord the full
amount owing* on or before March 8, 2022, the Tenant
will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from March 9, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
