Rajasundary Luxmanaperumal and Mohanathasan Subramaniam (the 'Landlords') applied
for an order determining that
Janine Canning (the 'Tenant') abandoned the rental unit.
 
This application was heard in Passcode:
956 0593 7653# on January
27, 2022. The Landlord’s
representatives, Susie Luxman and Nicole Fazzari, attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant has not been seen since September
18, 2021. The unit has been emptied
of the Tenant’s personal belongings.
 
2.     I find the Tenant abandoned the rental unit.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy between
the Landlords and the Tenant is terminated.
 
2.     The Tenant shall
also pay to the Landlords $186.00 for the cost of filing the application.
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlords the full amount
owing on or before February
11, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from February 12, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
