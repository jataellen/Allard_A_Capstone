Goalter De Mendonca Salwa Sattar Omar

Lubelia Silveira and Orlando
Silveira (the 'Landlords') applied for an order requiring Salwa Sattar Omar and
Goalter De Mendonca (the 'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard via videoconference on July 27, 2021 at 9:00 a.m.
 
Only Lubelia
Sivveira, one of the Landlords
attended the hearing and was self-represented. As of
10:59 a.m. the Tenants were not present or represented at the hearing though
being properly served with the Notice of Hearing by the Board.
The hearing proceeded without
the Tenants at 10:59 a.m.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.     This is a joint L3- Application to End a Tenancy and Evict a Tenant, Tenant
Gave Notice or Agreed to End
the Tenancy and a L9 Application to Collect Rent the Tenant Owes.
 
2.     The Tenants were in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.     The Landlord submitted that the Tenants vacated the
rental unit on March 14, 2021, returning the keys to
the rental unit to the Landlord and sending the Landlord a phone
“text” message confirming that they had vacated the rental unit.
 
4.     The monthly rent is
$2,900.00
 
5.    
The Landlord held a last month’s
rent deposit of $1,900.00 until November 1, 2020, This was
applied to November
rental charges pursuant
to an agreement to terminate tenancy effective December 1, 2020




File Number: TSL-19910-21
 
 
 
6.    
The Tenants did not pay the total
rent they were required to pay for the period from October 1, 2020 to March
31, 2021. The Landlord submitted
that the following
rental payments remain outstanding:
 
(a)  
October 2020            $    100.00
(b)  
November 2020        $1,000.00 ($1,900.00 last month rent deposit applied)
(c)  
December 2020        $2,900.00
(d)  
January 2021            $2,900.00
(e)  
February 2021          $2,900.00
(f)    
March 2021               $1,334.76 (Tenants
moved out March 14, 2021 –
in this case 14 days multiplied by a
per diem of $95.34)
 
It is
ordered that:
 
For the L3 - Application to End a Tenancy and Evict a Tenant, Tenant
Gave Notice or Agreed to End the Tenancy portion of
the application.
 
1.    
The tenancy between the Landlord and Tenants is terminated effective March 14, 2021, the day that the Tenants moved out of the rental
unit and returned
vacant possession of the rental unit to the Landlord.
 
For the L9 Application to Collect Rent the Tenant
Owes portion of the application.
 
2.    
The Tenants shall pay to the Landlords
$11,134.76, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to March 14, 2021
 
3.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlords $201.00
for the cost of filing
the application.
 
4.     If the Tenants do not pay the Landlords the full
amount owing on or before March 1, 2022, the Tenants
will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from March 2, 2022
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
