Order under Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: TNL-34019-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


302, 639 LAWRENCE AVENUE
WEST TORONTO ON M6A1A9


 




Between:


Lianzeng Yuan


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Thomas
Crown


Tenant



 
 
 
Lianzeng Yuan (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Thomas Crown (the 'Tenant') to pay
the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application
was heard by videoconference on November 22, 2021. Only the Landlord attended the hearing. As of 2:24 a.m., the Tenant was not present
or represented at the hearing although properly served with
notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
May 1, 2021 to July 31, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenant vacated the rental unit on
August 23, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the date the
application was filed.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent was $2,560.00.
 
4.    
The
Tenant has made no payments since the application was filed. However, the
Landlord applied the last month’s
rent deposit of $2,560.00,
to the month of August
2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $6,380.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to August 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant shall
also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: TNL-34019-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant
does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before January
27, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from January 28, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
