Order under Section 78(6)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

Between:


Tvm Division St Inc


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Miranda Oosterhof


Tenant



 
 
 
Tvm Division St Inc (the
'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Miranda Oosterhof (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant failed
to meet a condition specified
in the order issued by the
Board on December 8, 2021 with respect to application TEL-19039-21.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The order provided that the Landlord could apply to
the Board under section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act')
without notice to the Tenant
to terminate the tenancy and evict the Tenant if the Tenant
did not meet certain condition(s) specified in the
order.
 
2.     I find that the Tenant has not met the following
conditions specified in the order: The
Tenant failed to pay $450.00
towards the arrears,
on or before December 15, 2021. The Tenant failed to pay the lawful
monthly rent, on or before January 1, 2022.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy between
the Landlord and the Tenant
is terminated. The Tenant must move
out of the rental unit on or before February 12, 2022.
 
2.     If the unit is not vacated on or before
February 12, 2022, then starting
February 13, 2022, the Landlord may file this order
with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the eviction may be
enforced.
 
3.     Upon receipt of this order,
the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after February 13, 2022.
