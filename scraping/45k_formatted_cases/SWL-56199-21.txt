Landlord

Hari Thapa Manju

Tenants



 
Galaxy Value Add Ontario
Properties LP (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Hari Thapa
and Manju Basnet (the 'Tenants') to pay the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on February 3, 2022. The
Landlord's Legal Representative, Sara Mathew attended the hearing. As of 2:14 p.m., the Tenants were not
present or represented at the hearing although
properly served with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.      
The Tenants were in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenants did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period
from October 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenants shall
pay to the Landlord $1,370.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing
the application.
 
3.     If the Tenants
do not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before March 7, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe
interest. This will be simple
interest calculated from March 8, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance
outstanding.
