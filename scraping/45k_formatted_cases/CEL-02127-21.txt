Order under Section 78(6)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 



 


File Number: CEL-02127-21




In the matter
  of:


UPPER, 51 DOWNING CRESCENT BARRIE ON L4N8V6


 




Between:


Ryan Hawes


Landlord




 


and


 




 


Kevin Lonsdale


Tenant



 
On August 15, 2021, Ryan Hawes
(the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate the tenancy and
evict Kevin Lonsdale (the 'Tenant') and for an order to have the Tenant pay the
rent the Tenant owes because
the Tenant failed to meet a condition
specified in the order issued by the Board on July 19, 2021 with respect
to application CEL-99347-21.
 
Determinations:
 
1. Order CEL-99347-21 issued on July 19, 2021 does not contain a provision under Section
78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006, where
the Landlord would be entitled to apply to the Board should the Tenant
fail to meet a condition specified in the order.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.       
The Landlord's application under section 78 of the Act is dismissed.
 
2.       
Order CEL-99347-21, issued on July 19, 2021 remains
in effect.
