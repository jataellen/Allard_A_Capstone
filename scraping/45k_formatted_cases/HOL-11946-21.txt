45 Charles Ltd (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Melissandre Narbonne (the 'Tenant')
because the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on December 21, 2021. The Landlord’s Agent, R. Bellissimo and the Landlord's Legal Representative, Christina Nastas attended the hearing. As of
9:56 a.m., the Tenant was not present
or represented at the hearing
although properly served with
notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     
The Tenant has not paid
the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period from April 1, 2020
to October 31, 2021. Because of the
arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of Termination effective August 25, 2021.
 
2.     
The Tenant
vacated the rental
unit on October 11, 2021. The Tenant was in possession
of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.     
The lawful
monthly rent was
$2,803.87.
 
4.     
The Tenant made no payments after the application was filed.
 
5.     
The Landlord
is not holding a last month's rent deposit.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     
The Tenancy is
terminated as of October 11, 2021, the date
the Tenant gave vacant possession of the rental unit to the Landlord.
 
2.     
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $21,270.17*, which represents the amount of rent
owing and compensation up to October 11, 2021.




File Number: HOL-11946-21
 
3.     
The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord
$186.00 for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.     
If the Tenant does not
pay the Landlord the full amount owing* on or before February 11, 2022, the
Tenant will start to owe interest. This
will be simple interest calculated from February 12, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
