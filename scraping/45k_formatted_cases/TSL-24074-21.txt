Tony Filipe (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate
the tenancy and evict Annitho Joseph and Danney Stephen (the
'Tenants') because the Tenants did not pay the rent that the Tenants owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on December 21, 2021. Only the Landlord's Legal Representative, Jimmy Gangadin, attended the hearing. As of 9:42 a.m., the Tenants were not
present or represented at the hearing although properly served with notice of
this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants have not
paid the total rent the Tenants were required to pay for the period from April
1, 2020 to December 31, 2021. Because
of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of Termination effective December
2, 2020.
 
2.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on June 30, 2021.
The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent was
$1,100.00.
 
4.    
The Landlord
is not holding a last month's rent deposit.
 
5.    
The Tenants
have made no payments since the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The tenancy is
terminated as of June 30, 2021, the date the Tenants gave vacant possession of
the rental unit to the Landlord.




File Number: TSL-24074-21
 
2.    
The Tenants shall pay
to the Landlord $16,500.00*, which represents the amount of rent owing and
compensation up to June 30, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenants
shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord the
full amount owing* on or before January 25, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe
interest. This will be simple
interest calculated from January 26, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance
outstanding.
