Metcap Living
Management Inc (the 'Landlord') applied
for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Audrey Laurence (the 'Tenant')
because the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 23, 2021.
Only the Landlord’s representative, T. Radam, attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant
was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenant
vacated the rental unit on September 30, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
August 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
 
4.    
The Landlord
collected a rent deposit of $1,235.00 from the Tenant
and this deposit
is still being held by the
Landlord.
 
5.    
Interest on the rent deposit is owing to the Tenant for the period from January 1, 2021 to August 29, 2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to
the Landlord $1,293.97, which represents the amount of rent owing and compensation up to
September 30, 2021, less the rent deposit
and interest the Landlord owes on the rent deposit.
 
2.    
The Tenant
shall also pay to the Landlord $186.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SOL-24379-21
 
 
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before January
22, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from January 23, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
