Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: TNL-33867-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


208, 11 SHALLMAR BOULEVARD TORONTO ON M5N1J6


 




Between:


Beaux Properties Management Company


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Maria Fernanda Adan


Tenant



 
 
 
Beaux Properties Management
Company (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Maria
Fernanda Adan (the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 8, 2021.
Only the Landlord’s representative, A.
Metaxas, attended the hearing. It is determined that:
1.    
The Tenant has not paid
the total rent the Tenant
was required to pay for the period
from December 1, 2020 to November
30, 2021. Because
of the arrears, the Landlord
served a Notice of
Termination.
 
2.    
The total amount owing is $2,861.00.
 
3.     The Landlord requested
a conditional order based on an ongoing
payment plan agreed upon by the parties.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenant shall pay the Landlord $2,861.00 as follows:
 
a) $2,000.00 (rent plus $325.00) on the 8th day of each month
until the arrears
are fully paid.




File Number: TNL-33867-21
 
2.    
If the Tenant fails to make any
one of the payments in accordance with this order, the outstanding balance of
any arrears of rent and costs to be paid by the Tenant to the Landlord pursuant
to paragraph 1 of this order shall become immediately due and owing and the Landlord may, without notice to the Tenant apply to the Board
pursuant to section 78 of the Act for an order terminating the tenancy and
evicting the Tenant and requiring that the Tenant pay any new arrears, NSF fees
and related charges.
