Order under Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: TNL-29941-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


1008, 105 ROWENA DR TORONTO ON M3A1R2


 




Between:


1175326 Ontario Limited


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Devashish Tezpal Kunal Bhadury


Tenants



 
 
 
1175326 Ontario
Limited (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring
Devashish Tezpal and Kunal Bhadury (the 'Tenants') to pay
the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard
by videoconference on December
13, 2021. Only the Landlord’s Legal Representative, Bryan Rubin,
attended the hearing. The Tenants
were not present
or represented at the hearing
although properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenants
did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period
from May 1, 2020 to November
30, 2020.
 
2.    
The Tenants
vacated the rental
unit on December 31, 2020. The Tenants were in
possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
3.    
The lawful
monthly rent was $2,049.57.
 
4.    
The Landlord
applied the last month’s rent deposit against
the rent for December 2021.
 
5.     The Tenants paid $4,018.99, after the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$606.44, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to December 31, 2020.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: TNL-29941-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord the
full amount owing on or before
January 16, 2022, the Tenants
will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from January 17,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
