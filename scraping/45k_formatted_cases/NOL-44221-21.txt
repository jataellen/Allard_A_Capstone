Order under
Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: NOL-44221-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


302,
  27 TERRY FOX PLACE SAULT STE MARIE
ON P6A6M9


 




Between:


Skyline Living


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Silvie Larocque


Tenant



 
 
 
Skyline Living
(the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Silvie
Larocque (the 'Tenant') to pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
The Landlord also claimed administration charges related to NSF
cheques.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on January 20, 2022.
Only the Landlord’s agent,
Sandi Robertson, attended the hearing. As of 3:26 p.m., the Tenant was not
present or represented at the hearing although properly served with notice of
this hearing by the Board.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenant is in possession of the rental
unit.
 
3.     The monthly rent is $1,275.51.
 
4.     The Tenant has made no payments since
the application was filed.
 
5.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
September 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
6.    
The Landlord incurred charges
of $20.00 for administration charges
related to cheques tendered by or on behalf of the
Tenant, which were returned NSF.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $2,525.51, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.




File Number: NOL-44221-21
 
2.    
The Tenant shall also pay to the
Landlord $20.00, which
represents the total charges
related to NSF cheques tendered to the Landlord by or on behalf of the Tenant
 
3.     The Tenant shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.
 
4.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before November
11, 2021, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from November 12, 2021
at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
