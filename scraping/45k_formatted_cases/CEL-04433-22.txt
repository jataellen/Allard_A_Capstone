On January 10, 2022, Mariposa Place
(Orillia) Non-Profit Homes Corp (the 'Landlord') applied
in this L4 application for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict
Crystal Schwemler (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant failed to meet a condition
specified in the previous consent order issued by the Board on December 3, 2020
with respect to L1/L2 application CEL-94126-20.
 
Determinations:
 
1.       
The consent order provided that
the Landlord could apply to the Board under section 78 of the Residential
Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act') without notice to the Tenant to terminate the tenancy and evict the Tenant if the Tenant
did not meet certain condition(s) specified in the consent
order.
 
2.       
I
find that the Tenant has not met the following
condition specified in the consent
order:
 
The Tenant failed to pay to the Landlord lawful
rent on or before January 1, 2022. This was a breach
of paragraph 3 of the consent order.
This L4 application as filed within 30 days of the breach.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.       
The previous consent order CEL-94126-20 is cancelled.
 
2.       
The tenancy between the Landlord and the Tenant
is terminated. The Tenant must move
out of the rental unit on or before February 1, 2022 (standard 11 days from the issuance date of this
order).
 
3.       
If the unit is not vacated
on or before February 1, 2022, then starting February
2, 2022, the Landlord may
file this order with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the
eviction may be enforced.




File Number: CEL-04433-22
 
4.       
Upon receipt of this order,
the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after February 2, 2022.
