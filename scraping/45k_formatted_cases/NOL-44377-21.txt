Laura Kingsley (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring William Cargill (the 'Tenant') to pay
the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on February 3, 2022. Only the Landlord, Laura Kingsley, attended
the hearing. As of 2:32 p.m., the Tenant was not present
or represented at the
hearing although properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.      
The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant
was required to pay for the period
from October 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenant vacated the rental unit on October
31, 2021. The Tenant was in possession of the rental unit on the date
the application was filed.
 
4.     The monthly rent is $1,000.00.
 
5.     The Landlord is not holding
a last month's rent deposit.
 
6.     The Tenant has made no payments since the application was filed.
 
 
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $1,000.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.




File Number: NOL-44377-21
 
2.     The Tenant shall
also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount owing on or before
February 22, 2022, the Tenant
will start to owe interest. This will
be simple interest calculated from February 23, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
