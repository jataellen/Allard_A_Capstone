Seaforth Manor Retirement Residence (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring
Connie Blake (the 'Tenant')
to pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on December 16, 2021. Only the Landlord's Representative,
Michael Biasiucci, attended the hearing. As
of 2:21 p.m., the Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing although
properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Preliminary Issue:
 
The amount outstanding
for the period ending December 31, 2021
($35,458.89 inclusive of
costs) exceeds the $35,000.00 monetary jurisdiction of the Board. The Landlord
was informed, and agreed that by proceeding with this application, all rights
the Landlord may have in excess of the Board’s monetary jurisdiction are
extinguished on issuance of this order.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The Tenant did not pay
the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period from July 1, 2019
to December 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant is in possession of the rental
unit.
 
3.     The lawful monthly
rent is $2,791.15.
 
4.     The Tenant paid $25,645.39 after the application was filed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant
shall pay to the Landlord $35,000.00, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to December 31, 2021 and the cost of filing the application.




File Number: SWL-48090-21
 
2.     If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord the
full amount owing on or before January 17, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe
interest. This will be simple
interest calculated from January 18, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance
outstanding.
