Residential Tenancies
Act, 2006

  matter of:


915, 111 RIVERSIDE DRIVE

Between:


Lisa Wohlers-Estate Trustee Estate of Constance Slavik


Landlords




 


 
and


 




 


Garry Sebastiany


Tenant



 
 
Lisa Wohlers- Estate Trustee
and the Estate of Constance
Slavik (the 'Landlords') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and
evict Garry Sebastiany (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant gave a notice to
terminate the tenancy.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant gave the Landlords
a notice to terminate his tenancy effective February 28, 2022.
 
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The tenancy between
the Landlords and the Tenant is terminated. The Tenant must move out of the rental unit on or
before March 6, 2022.
 
2.     If the unit is not vacated on or before
March 6, 2022,
then starting March
7, 2022, the Landlords may file this order with the
Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the eviction may be enforced.
 
 
3.    
Upon receipt of this order, the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlords on or after March 7, 2022.
