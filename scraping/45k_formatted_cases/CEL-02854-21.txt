Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: CEL-02854-21
 
 



In the matter
of:


A, 30 INDIANOLA
  CRESCENT WASAGA BEACH ON L9Z2W3


 




Between:


Eagles Luxury
Homes Inc.


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Cindy Henry Victoria Boomhower


Tenants



 
 
 
Eagles Luxury Homes Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict
Cindy Henry (CH) and Victoria Boomhower (the 'Tenants') because the Tenants did
not pay the rent that the Tenants owe.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on January
10, 2022.
 
The Landlord’s representative, Edwin Pula, and the Tenant,
CH, attended the hearing.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The notice of termination does not identify
the rental unit for which
the notice was given
as required by s. 43(1)(a) of the Act.
 
2.     As a result,
the Landlord’s application must be dismissed
as the notice of termination is invalid.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Landlord's application is dismissed.
