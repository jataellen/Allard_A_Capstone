King East Developments Inc. (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring Fatma Aksahan and Adem Aksahan (the 'Tenants') to pay
the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by
way of video conference on January 10, 2022. Only the Landlord’s legal representative Howard
Levenson attended the hearing. As of
3:08pm the Tenant was not present despite being served with notice of hearing
from the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.      
The Tenants were in possession of the rental unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenants did not pay the total
rent they were required to pay for the period from
September 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
 
3.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on September 21, 2021.
 
4.     The Tenant made no payments
since the application was filed.
 
5.     The Landlord is not holding
a last month’s rent deposit.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants shall pay to the Landlord $1,242.73, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to September 21, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing
the application.




File Number: TNL-34820-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before February
25, 2022, the Tenants
will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from February 26,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
