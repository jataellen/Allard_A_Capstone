On November 27, 2021, Rodney
Desouza (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Leopoldo
Aka Leo Gallo
(the 'Tenant') and for an order to have the Tenant pay the rent the Tenant owes because the
Tenant failed to meet a condition specified in the order issued by the Board on
October 8, 2021 with respect to application HOL-08808-20 RV-AM.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
The order provided that the
Landlord could apply to the Board under section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the
'Act') without notice
to the Tenant to terminate
the tenancy and evict the
Tenant if the Tenant did not
meet certain condition(s) specified
in the order.
 
2.    
I
find that the Tenant has not met the following
condition(s) specified in the order:
 
On
or before October 19, 2021, the Tenant was to ensure that the outstanding water
account is in good standing in accordance with the requirements of the terms of
his lease and that all payment requirements of the lease regarding the payment of the monthly
water utility charges
are complied with, in full and on time
for payments for each and every month, for a twelve (12) month period.
 
On
November 1, 2021 and November 10, 2021, the Landlords delivered to the Tenant, the Township of King water
and sewer bill for the amount of $603.69 due on November 15, 2021. As of November 16, 2021, the Tenant had
failed to pay
$603.69 towards
the water and sewer.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
Order HOL-08808-20 RV-AM is
cancelled.
 
2.     The tenancy between
the Landlord and the Tenant
is terminated. The Tenant must move
out of the rental unit on or before February 15, 2022.




File Number: CEL-04500-22
 
3.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $190.68
per day for compensation for the use of the unit starting February 5, 2022 to the
date the Tenant moves out of the unit.
 
4.     If the unit is not vacated on or before February 15, 2022, then starting February
16, 2022, the Landlord may
file this order with the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) so that the
eviction may be enforced.
 
5.     Upon receipt of this order,
the Court Enforcement Office (Sheriff) is directed to give
vacant possession of the unit to the Landlord on or after February 16, 2022.
