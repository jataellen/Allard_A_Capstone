Teresa Willoughby


Tenant



 
 
Cityhousing Hamilton
Corporation (the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy
and evict Teresa Willoughby (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant did not pay the
rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by
videoconference on February 1, 2022. Only the Landlord’s Legal Representative,
Sharon Churcher, attended the hearing. As of 2:37 p.m., the Tenant was not
present or represented at the hearing although
properly served with notice of this hearing
by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant has not paid the total rent the Tenant was required to pay for the period
from March 1, 2021 to February 28, 2022. Because of the arrears, the Landlord served a Notice of
Termination effective March 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant is in possession of the rental
unit.
 
3.     As of November
1, 2022, the monthly rent is $222.00.
 
4.     The Tenant paid all arrears
for the period ending January
1, 2022 after the application was filed.
 
5.     The only amount outstanding is a portion
the Landlord's cost of filing
the application. As a
result, the order will be limited to costs only and not eviction.
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.     The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord
$184.00 for the remaining cost of filing
the application.




File Number: SOL-25434-21
 
2.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount owing* on or before
February 18, 2022, the Tenant
will start to owe interest. This will
be simple interest calculated from February 19, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
