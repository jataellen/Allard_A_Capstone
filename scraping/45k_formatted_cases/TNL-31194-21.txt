Order under Subsection 87(1)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006

In the matter
of:


242 WILLIAMSON ROAD MARKHAM ON L6E1R9


 




Between:


Haoqi Xiang


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Richard Racz Richardne Racz


Tenants



 
 
 
Haoqi Xiang
(the 'Landlord') applied
for an order requiring Richardne Racz and Richard
Racz (the 'Tenants') to pay
the rent that they owe.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 8, 2021.
Only the Landlord’s representative, R. W. Yu, attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenants were in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenants vacated
the rental unit on February
18, 2021.
 
3.    
The Tenants
did not pay the total rent they were required
to pay for the period from
October 1, 2019 to February 18, 2021.
 
4.     The Tenants have been
credited with the rent deposit
in the amount of $1,980.00.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenants
shall pay to the Landlord
$29,447.28, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to February 28, 2021, less the rent deposit.
 
2.     The Tenants shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: TNL-31194-21
 
3.    
If the Tenants do not
pay the Landlord the full amount owing on or before January 18, 2022, the Tenants will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest
calculated from January 19,
2022 at 2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
