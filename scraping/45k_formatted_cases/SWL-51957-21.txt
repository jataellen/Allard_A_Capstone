Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies
Act, 2006
 
File Number: SWL-51957-21
 
 



In the
  matter of:


3B, 3174 WALKER ROAD WINDSOR ON N8W3R5


 




Between:


Hirev (Windsor) Inc.


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Hassan Rubba


Tenant



 
 
 
Hirev (Windsor) Inc. (the
'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Hassan Rubba
(the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by video conference on January 13, 2022.
 
The Landlord’s Legal Representative, Olaniya Afolabi, and the Tenant attended the hearing.
 
 
Determinations:
 
1.     At the hearing,
the Landlord’s Legal Representative could not confirm
the actual amount of rental arrears owing.
 
2.    
The Tenant claimed many more rental payments were made than the Landlord’s Legal Representative could account for.
 
3.     Neither party could confirm
or prove their respective claims.
 
4.     Based on the evidence presented at the hearing, I
allowed the Landlord’s Legal Representative
to withdraw the application in accordance with subsection 200(4)
of the Act.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.     The Landlord's application is dismissed.
