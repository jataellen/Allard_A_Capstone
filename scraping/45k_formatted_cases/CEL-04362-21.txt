1051/1061 Seneca Avenue Apts.
(the 'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict
Michele Ziraldo (the 'Tenant') and for an order to have the Tenant pay the rent
the Tenant owes because
the Tenant failed to meet a condition
specified in the order issued by the Board on December 9, 2021 with respect
to application CEL-02105-21.
 
 
Determinations:
 
 
1.     The Board issued an amended order pertaining to file
number CEL-02105-21-AM on January 17, 2022 which required
the Tenant to make a regularly
scheduled payment to the Landlord that was to be applied
against outstanding rental arrears and costs.
 
 
2.    
The agreement provided that the
Landlord could apply to the Board under section 78 of the Residential
Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act') without notice to the Tenants to terminate
the tenancy and evict the Tenants if the Tenants
did not meet certain conditions specified in the order.
 
 
3.     Pursuant to subsection 78(1), paragraph 3 of the Act, an application made under section 78 can be made in the event the
tenant has not met one or more of the conditions described in the order.
 
 
4.     As the Tenant
has met all of the conditions outlined
in the order, the application is dismissed.




File Number: CEL-04362-21
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.     The Landlord's application under section 78 of the Act is dismissed.
