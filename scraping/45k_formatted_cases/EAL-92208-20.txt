Order under Section 69
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: EAL-92208-20
 
 



In the matter
of:


112 JOBIN AVENUE E CORNWALL ON K6J3L1


 




Between:


Luc Desormeaux Janine Desormeaux


Landlords




 


 
and


 




 


Albert Jr Marsolais Christine Grignon


Tenants



 
 
 
Luc Desormeaux and Janine Desormeaux (the 'Landlords') applied
for an order to terminate
the tenancy and evict Christine Grignon
and Albert Jr Marsolais (the 'Tenants') because
they intend to demolish the
rental unit.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 3, 2021.
The Landlords and the Tenants attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.    The
Landlords do not intend to carry out the demolition. The Landlords decided
to sell the residential complex but have not filed an application to
terminate the tenancy and evict the Tenants for purchaser’s own use.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    The Landlords' application is dismissed.
