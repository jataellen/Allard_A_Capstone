Skyline Living (the 'Landlord') applied for an order requiring Peter Biro (the 'Tenant') to pay the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on January 27, 2022. Only the Landlord’s Agent, Sandi Robertson-Brooks, attended the hearing. As of 2:45
p.m., the Tenant was not present or represented at the hearing although
properly served with notice of this hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.     The Tenant vacated
the rental unit on October
31, 2021.
 
3.     The monthly rent was $848.26.
 
4.     The Landlord is not holding
a last month's rent deposit.
 
5.     The Tenant has made no payments since the application was filed.
 
6.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant
was required to pay for the period
from July 1, 2021 to October 31, 2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord $3,093.04, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to October 31, 2021.
 
2.     The Tenant shall
also pay to the Landlord
$201.00 for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: NOL-44286-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount owing on or before
February 15, 2022, the Tenant
will start to owe interest. This will
be simple interest calculated from February 16, 2022 at 2.00% annually on the
balance outstanding.
