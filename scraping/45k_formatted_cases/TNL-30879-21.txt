Landlord

Justin
Issac Spears


Tenant



 
 
 
Nubury Properties Limited (the 'Landlord') applied for an order
requiring Justin Issac
Spears (the 'Tenant') to pay
the rent that the Tenant owes.
 
This application was heard by videoconference on November 8, 2021.
Only the Landlord’s representative, S. Ursino, attended the hearing.
Determinations:
 
1.      
The Tenant was in possession of the rental
unit on the date the application was filed.
 
2.    
The Tenant did not pay the total rent the Tenant was required
to pay for the period from
April 1, 2020 to February 28, 2021.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    
The Tenant
shall pay to the Landlord
$4,593.20, which represents the amount of rent
owing up to February 28, 2021.
 
2.    
The Tenant
shall also pay to the Landlord $201.00
for the cost of filing the application.




File Number: TNL-30879-21
 
3.    
If the Tenant does not pay the Landlord
the full amount
owing on or before January
18, 2022, the Tenant will start to owe interest. This will be simple interest calculated from January 19, 2022 at
2.00% annually on the balance outstanding.
