Red Tree Developments (the
'Landlord') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict
Crystal-Lynn Beland (the 'Tenant') because the Tenant failed to meet a
condition specified in the order issued by the Board on January
6, 2022 and amended
on February 8, 2022, with respect to application EAL-95073-21.
 
Determination:
 
1.    Order EAL-95073-21, issued on January
6, 2022 and amended on February 8, 2022, does not provide that the Landlord can
apply to the LTB under section 78 of the Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (the 'Act').
Therefore, this application shall be dismissed.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    The Landlord's application under section 78 of the Act is dismissed.
