Landlords




 
 
and
 
Silvia Cunha Barraclough                                                                                                                 Tenant
 
 
Tharshini Perumainar and
Perumainar Visvalingam (the 'Landlords') applied for an order to terminate the tenancy and evict Silvia Cunha Barraclough (the 'Tenant') because
the Tenant did not pay the rent that the Tenant
owes.
 
On October 21, 2021 the Board
issued order TNL-33348-21 terminating the tenancy
and evicting the Tenant.
 
The Tenant filed a motion to void the order.
 
This motion was heard by video conference on January 7, 2022, beginning at 1:00 p.m..
 
The Tenant Silvia Cunha Barraclough attended
the hearing. As of 2:15 p.m., the Landlords were not present or represented at this
hearing, although properly served with a notice of the hearing by the Board.
 
Determinations:
 
1.    
In Order TNL-33348-21, the Board set out that to void the order,
the amount of $12,936.00
must be paid to either the Landlords or the Board on or before November 15,
2021. This amount represents all the rent that is in arrears under the tenancy
agreement, any additional rent that would have been owing under the tenancy
agreement on the date of the Tenant's payment, and any costs ordered by the
Board.
 
2.     
On November 11, 2021, the Tenant
paid to the Landlords the full amount
owing of
$12,936.00.
 
It is ordered that:
 
1.    Order TNL-33348-21 is void under subsection 74(4) of the Act.
