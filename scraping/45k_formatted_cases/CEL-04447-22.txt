Order under Section 78(6)
Residential Tenancies Act, 2006
 
File Number: CEL-04447-22
 
 



In the matter
of:


57
  MIDDLETON WAY BRAMPTON ON L6S4B1


 




Between:


Peel Housing
Corp. O/A Peel Living


Landlord




 


 
and


 




 


Claudine Gregory


Tenant



 
 
Peel Housing Corp. O/A Peel
Living (the 'Landlord') applied for an order
to terminate the tenancy and evict
Claudine Gregory (the 'Tenant') and for an order to have the Tenant pay the
rent he owes because the Tenant failed to meet a condition
specified in the order issued by the Board on October 28, 2020 with respect to
application CEL-92871-20.
 
Determinations:
 
1.     The Landlord filed their application with the Board via email
on January 12, 2022.
 
2.    
The final payment in the previous order was to be made to the Landlord
on or before October 20, 2021.
 
3.    
The application was not filed with the Board within 30 days of any potential
breach in the order and as a result cannot move
forward.
 
It is
ordered that:
 
1.     The Landlord's application under section 78 of the Act is dismissed.
 
2.     Order CEL-92871-20, issued
on October 28, 2020 remains
in effect.
